HYMN XXV. BRAHMAṆASPATI.

इन्धानोindhāno अग्निंaghniṃ वनवदvanavad वनुष्यतःvanuṣyataḥ कर्तब्रह्माkṛtabrahmā शूशुवदśūśuvad रातहव्यrātahavya इतit ||
HE lighting up the flame shall conquer enemies: strong shall he be who offers prayer and brings his gift.
He with his seed spreads forth beyond another's seed, whomever Brahmaṇaspati takes for his friend.
2.25.1
जातेनjātena जातमतिjātamati सsa परpra सर्स्र्तेsarsṛte यं-यंyaṃ-yaṃ युजंक्र्णुतेyujaṃkṛṇute बरह्मणसbrahmaṇas पतिःpatiḥ ||
With heroes he shall overcome his hero foes, and spread his wealth by kine wise by himself is be.
His children and his children's children grow in strength, whomever Brahmaṇaspati takes for his friend.
2.25.2
वीरेभिर्वीरानvīrebhirvīrān वनवदvanavad वनुष्यतोvanuṣyato गोभीghobhī रयिंrayiṃ पप्रथदpaprathad बोधतिbodhati तमनाtmanā |

तोकंtokaṃ चca तस्यtasya तनयंtanayaṃ चca वर्धतेvardhate यं-यंyaṃ-yaṃ ...... ||
He, mighty like a raving river's billowy flood, as a bull conquers oxen, overcomes with strength.
Like Agni's blazing rush he may not be restrained, whomever Brahmaṇaspati takes for his friend.
2.25.3
सिन्धुर्नsindhurna कषोदःkṣodaḥ शिमीवानśimīvān रघायतोṛghāyato वर्षेवvṛṣeva वध्रीन्रभिvadhrīnrabhi वष्ट्योजसाvaṣṭyojasā |

अग्नेरिवaghneriva परसितिर्नाहprasitirnāha वर्तवेvartave यं-यंyaṃ-yaṃ .... ||
For him the floods of heaven flow never failing down: first with the heroes he goes forth to war for kine.
He slays in unabated vigour with great might, whomever Brahmaṇaspati takes for his friend.
2.25.4
तस्माtasmā अर्षन्तिarṣanti दिव्याdivyā असश्चतःasaścataḥ सsa सत्वभिःsatvabhiḥ परथमोprathamo गोषुगछतिghoṣughachati |

अनिभ्र्ष्टतविषिर्हन्त्योजसाanibhṛṣṭataviṣirhantyojasā यं-यंyaṃ-yaṃ ...... ||
All roaring rivers pour their waters down for him, and many a flawless shelter hath been granted him.
Blest with the happiness of Gods he prospers well, whomever Brahmaṇaspati takes for his friend.
2.25.5
तस्माtasmā इदid विश्वेviśve धुनयन्तdhunayanta सिन्धवो.अछिद्राsindhavo.achidrā शर्मśarma दधिरेdadhire पुरूणिpurūṇi |
