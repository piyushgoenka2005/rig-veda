HYMN XXIX. VIŚVEDEVAS.

धर्तव्रताdhṛtavratā आदित्याādityā इषिराiṣirā आरेāre मतmat कर्तkarta रहसूरिवागःrahasūrivāghaḥ |

शर्ण्वतोśṛṇvato वोvo वरुणvaruṇa मित्रmitra देवाdevā भद्रस्यbhadrasya विद्वानवसेvidvānavase हुवेवःhuvevaḥ ||
UPHOLDERS of the Law, ye strong Ādityas, remove my sin like her who bears in secret.
You, Varuṇa, Mitra and all Gods who listen, I call to help me, I who know your goodness.
2.29.1
यूयंyūyaṃ देवाःdevāḥ परमतिर्यूयमोजोpramatiryūyamojo यूयंyūyaṃ दवेषांसिdveṣāṃsi सनुतर्युयोतsanutaryuyota |

अभिक्षत्तारोabhikṣattāro अभिabhi चca कषमध्वमद्याkṣamadhvamadyā चca नोno मर्ळयतापरंmṛḷayatāparaṃ चca ||
Ye, Gods, are providence and ye are power: remove ye utterly all those who hate us.
As givers of good things deal with us kindly: this day be gracious to us and hereafter.
2.29.2
किमूkimū नुnu वःvaḥ कर्णवामापरेणkṛṇavāmāpareṇa किंkiṃ सनेनsanena वसवvasava आप्येनāpyena |

यूयंyūyaṃ नोno मित्रावरुणादितेmitrāvaruṇādite चca सवस्तिमिन्द्रामरुतोsvastimindrāmaruto दधातdadhāta ||
What service may we do you with our future, what service, Vasus, with our ancient friendship?
O Aditi, and Varuṇa and Mitra, Indra and Maruts, make us well and happy.
2.29.3
हयेhaye देवाdevā यूयमिदापयyūyamidāpaya सथstha तेte मर्ळतmṛḷata नाधमानायnādhamānāya मह्यमmahyam |

माmā वोvo रथोratho मध्यमवाळmadhyamavāḷ रतेṛte भूनbhūn माmā युष्मावस्त्वापिषुyuṣmāvastvāpiṣu शरमिष्मśramiṣma ||
Ye, O ye Gods, are verily our kinsmen as such be kind to me who now implore you.
Let not your car come slowly to our worship: of kinsmen such as you ne’er let us weary.
2.29.4
परpra वva एकोeko मिमयmimaya भूर्यागोbhūryāgho यनyan माmā पितेवpiteva कितवंkitavaṃ शशासśaśāsa |

आरेāre पाषाpāṣā आरेāre अघानिaghāni देवाdevā माmā माधिmādhi पुत्रेputre विमिवvimiva गरभीष्टghrabhīṣṭa ||
I singly have sinned many a sin against you, and ye chastised me as a sire the gambler.
Far be your nets, far, Gods, be mine offences: seize me not like a bird upon her offspring.
2.29.5
अर्वाञ्चोarvāñco अद्याadyā भवताbhavatā यजत्राyajatrā आā वोvo हार्दिhārdi भयमानोbhayamāno वययेयमvyayeyam |

तराध्वंtrādhvaṃ नोno देवाdevā निजुरोnijuro वर्कस्यvṛkasya तराध्वंtrādhvaṃ कर्तादवपदोkartādavapado यजत्राःyajatrāḥ ||
Turn yourselves hitherward this day, ye Holy, that fearing in my heart I may approach you.
Protect us, God; let not the wolf destroy us. Save us, ye Holy, from the pit and falling.
2.29.6
माहंmāhaṃ मघोनोmaghono ...... ||
May I not live, O Varuṇa, to witness my wealthy, liberal, dear friend's destitution.
King, may I never lack well-ordered riches. Loud may we speak, with heroes, in assembly.
2.29.7
