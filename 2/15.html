HYMN XV. INDRA

परpra घाghā नवस्यnvasya महतोmahato महानिmahāni सत्याsatyā सत्यस्यsatyasya करणानिkaraṇāni वोचमvocam |

तरिकद्रुकेश्वपिबतtrikadrukeśvapibat सुतस्यास्यsutasyāsya मदेmade अहिमिन्द्रोahimindro जघानjaghāna ||
Now, verily, will I declare the exploits, mighty and true, of him the True and Mighty.
In the Trikadrukas he drank the Soma: then in its rapture Indra slew the Dragon.
2.15.1
अवंशेavaṃśe दयामस्तभायदdyāmastabhāyad बर्हन्तमाbṛhantamā रोदसीrodasī अप्र्णदन्तरिक्षमapṛṇadantarikṣam |

सsa धारयदdhārayad पर्थिवींpṛthivīṃ पप्रथच्चpaprathacca सोमस्यsomasya ताtā मदmada इन्द्रश्चकारindraścakāra ||
High heaven unsupported in space he stablished: he filled the two worlds and the air's mid-region.
Earth he upheld, and gave it wide expansion. These things did Indra in the Soma's rapture.
2.15.2
सद्मेवsadmeva पराचोprāco विvi मिमायmimāya मानैर्वज्रेणmānairvajreṇa खान्यत्र्णनkhānyatṛṇan नदीनामnadīnām |

वर्थास्र्जतvṛthāsṛjat पथिभिर्दीर्घयाथैःpathibhirdīrghayāthaiḥ सोमस्यsomasya ताtā ...... ||
From front, as ’twere a house, he ruled and measured; pierced with his bolt the fountains of the rivers,
And made them flow at ease by paths far-reaching, These things did Indra in the Soma's rapture.
2.15.3
सsa परवोळ्हॄनpravoḷhṝn परिगत्याparighatyā दभीतेर्विश्वमधागायुधमिद्धेdabhīterviśvamadhāghāyudhamiddhe अग्नौaghnau |

संsaṃ गोभिरश्वैरस्र्जदghobhiraśvairasṛjad रथेभिःrathebhiḥ सो...so... ||
Compassing those who bore away Dabhīti, in kindled fire he burnt up all their weapons.
And made him rich with kine and cars and horses. These things did Indra in the Soma's rapture.
2.15.4
सsa ईंīṃ महींmahīṃ धुनिमेतोररम्णातdhunimetoraramṇāt सोso अस्नातॄनपारयतasnātṝnapārayat सवस्तिsvasti |

तta उत्स्नायutsnāya रयिमभिrayimabhi परpra तस्थुःtasthuḥ सो...so... ||
The mighty roaring flood he stayed from flowing, and carried those who swam not safely over.
They having crossed the stream attained to riches. These things did Indra in the Soma's rapture.
2.15.5
सोदञ्चंsodañcaṃ सिन्धुमरिणानsindhumariṇān महित्वाmahitvā वज्रेणानvajreṇāna उषसःuṣasaḥ संsaṃ पिपेषpipeṣa |

अजवसोajavaso जविनीभिर्विव्र्श्चनjavinībhirvivṛścan सो...so... ||
With mighty power he made the stream flow upward, crushed with his thunderbolt the car of Uṣas,
Rending her slow steeds with his rapid coursers. These things did Indra in the Soma's rapture.
2.15.6
सsa विद्वानपगोहंvidvānapaghohaṃ कनीनामाविर्भवन्नुदतिष्ठतkanīnāmāvirbhavannudatiṣṭhat पराव्र्कparāvṛk |

परतिprati शरोणśroṇa सथादsthād वयनगचष्टvyanaghacaṣṭa सो...so... ||
Knowing the place wherein the maids were hiding, the outcast showed himself and stood before them.
The cripple stood erect, the blind beheld them. These things did Indra in the Soma's rapture.
2.15.7
भिनदbhinad वलमङगिरोभिर्ग्र्णानोvalamaṅghirobhirghṛṇāno विvi पर्वतस्यparvatasya दरंहितान्यैरतdṛṃhitānyairat |

रिणगriṇagh रोधांसिrodhāṃsi कर्त्रिमाण्येषांkṛtrimāṇyeṣāṃ सो...so... ||
Praised by the Aṅgirases he slaughtered Vala, and burst apart the bulwarks of the mountain.
He tore away their deftly-built defences. These things did Indra in the Soma's rapture.
2.15.8
सवप्नेनाभ्युप्याsvapnenābhyupyā चुमुरिंcumuriṃ धुनिंdhuniṃ चca जघन्थjaghantha दस्यंdasyaṃ परpra दभीतिमावःdabhītimāvaḥ |

रम्भीrambhī चिदत्रcidatra विविदेvivide हिरण्यंhiraṇyaṃ सो...so... ||
Thou, with sleep whelming Cumuri and Dhuni, slewest the Dasyu, keptest safe Dabhīti.
There the staff-bearer found the golden treasure. These things did Indra in the Soma's rapture.
2.15.9
नूनंnūnaṃ साsā तेte परतिprati ...... ||
Now let that wealthy Cow of thine, O Indra , yield in return a boon to him who lauds thee.
Give to thy praisers: let not fortune fail us. Loud may we speak, with brave men, in assembly.
