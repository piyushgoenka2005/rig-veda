HYMN XXXVII INDRA.

अर्वागarvāgh रथंrathaṃ विश्ववारंviśvavāraṃ तta उग्रेन्द्रughrendra युक्तासोyuktāso हरयोharayo वहन्तुvahantu |

कीरिश्चिदkīriścid धिdhi तवाtvā हवतेhavate सवर्वानsvarvān रधीमहिṛdhīmahi सधमादस्तेद्यsadhamādasteadya ||
LET thy Bay Horses, yoked, O mighty Indra, bring thy car hither fraught with every blessing.
For thee, the Heavenly, e’en the poor invoketh: may we this day, thy feast-companions, prosper.
6.37.1
परोpro दरोणेdroṇe हरयःharayaḥ कर्माग्मनkarmāghman पुनानासpunānāsa रज्यन्तोṛjyanto अभूवनabhūvan |

इन्द्रोindro नोno अस्यasya पूर्व्यःpūrvyaḥ पपीयादpapīyād दयुक्षोdyukṣo मदस्यmadasya सोम्यस्यsomyasya राजाrājā ||
Forth to the vat the brown drops flow for service, and purified proceed directly forward.
May Indra drink of this, our guest aforetime, Celestial King of the strong draught of Soma.
6.37.2
आसस्राणासःāsasrāṇāsaḥ शवसानमछेन्द्रंśavasānamachendraṃ सुचक्रेsucakre रथ्यासोrathyāso अश्वाःaśvāḥ |

अभिabhi शरवśrava रज्यन्तोṛjyanto वहेयुर्नूvaheyurnū चिनcin नुnu वायोरम्र्तंvāyoramṛtaṃ विदस्येतvidasyet ||
Bringing us hitherward all-potent Indra on well-wheeled chariot, may the Steeds who bear him
Convey him on the road direct to glory, and ne’er may Vāyu's Amṛta cease and fail him.
6.37.3
वरिष्ठोvariṣṭho अस्यasya दक्षिणामियर्तीन्द्रोdakṣiṇāmiyartīndro मघोनांmaghonāṃ तुविकूर्मितमःtuvikūrmitamaḥ |

ययाyayā वज्रिवःvajrivaḥ परियास्यंहोpariyāsyaṃho मघाmaghā चca धर्ष्णोdhṛṣṇo दयसेdayase विvi सूरीनsūrīn ||
Supreme, he stirs this man to give the guerdon,—Indra, most efficacious of the princes,—
Wherewith, O Thunderer, thou removest sorrow, and, Bold One! partest wealth among the nobles.
6.37.4
इन्द्रोindro वाजस्यvājasya सथविरस्यsthavirasya दातेन्द्रोdātendro गीर्भिर्वर्धतांghīrbhirvardhatāṃ वर्द्धमहाःvṛddhamahāḥ |

इन्द्रोindro वर्त्रंvṛtraṃ हनिष्ठोhaniṣṭho अस्तुastu सत्वाsatvā ताtā सूरिःsūriḥ पर्णतिpṛṇati तूतुजानःtūtujānaḥ ||
Indra is hewho gives enduring vigour: may our songs magnify the God Most Mighty.
Best Vṛtra-slayer be the Hero Indra these things he gives as Prince, with strong endeavour.
6.37.5
