HYMN XLIII. INDRA

यस्यyasya तयच्छम्बरंtyacchambaraṃ मदेmade दिवोदासायdivodāsāya रन्धयःrandhayaḥ |

अयंayaṃ सsa सोमsoma इन्द्रindra तेte सुतःsutaḥ पिबpiba ||
IN whose wild joy thou madest once Śambara Divodāsa's prey,
This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra: drink!
6.43.1
यस्यyasya तीव्रसुतंtīvrasutaṃ मदंmadaṃ मध्यमन्तंmadhyamantaṃ चca रक्षसेrakṣase |

अयंayaṃ स...sa... ||
Whose gladdening draught, shed from the points, thou guardest in the midst and end,
This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra drink!
6.43.2
यस्यyasya गाghā अन्तरश्मनोantaraśmano मदेmade दर्ळ्हाdṛḷhā अवास्र्जःavāsṛjaḥ |

अयंayaṃ सsa .... .. ||
In whose wild joy thou settest free the kine held fast within the rock,
This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra: drink!
6.43.3
यस्यyasya मन्दानोmandāno अन्धसोandhaso माघोनंmāghonaṃ दधिषेdadhiṣe शवःśavaḥ |

अयंayaṃ स...sa... ||
This, in whose juice delighting thou gainest the might of Maghavan,
This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra drink!
6.43.4
