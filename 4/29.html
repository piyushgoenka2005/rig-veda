HYMN XXIX. INDRA.

आā नna सतुतstuta उपupa वाजेभिरvājebhir ऊतीūtī इन्द्रindra याहिyāhi हरिभिरharibhir मन्दसानःmandasānaḥ |

तिरशtiraś चिदcid अर्यःaryaḥ सवनाsavanā पुरूण्यpurūṇy आङगूषेभिरāṅghūṣebhir गर्णानःghṛṇānaḥ सत्यराधाःsatyarādhāḥ ||
COME, lauded, unto us with powers and succours, O Indra, with thy Tawny Steeds; exulting,
Past even the foeman's manifold libations, glorified with our hymns, true Wealth-bestower.
4.29.1
आā हिhi षमाṣmā यातिyāti नर्यशnaryaś चिकित्वानcikitvān हूयमानःhūyamānaḥ सोत्र्भिरsotṛbhir उपupa यज्ञमyajñam |

सवश्वोsvaśvo योyo अभीरुरabhīrur मन्यमानःmanyamānaḥ सुष्वाणेभिरsuṣvāṇebhir मदतिmadati संsaṃ हha वीरैःvīraiḥ ||
Man's Friend, to this our sacrifice he cometh marking how he is called by Soma-pressers.
Fearless, and conscious that his Steeds are noble, he joyeth with the Soma-pouring heroes.
4.29.2
शरावयेदśrāvayed अस्यasya कर्णाkarṇā वाजयध्यैvājayadhyai जुष्टामjuṣṭām अनुanu परpra दिशमdiśam मन्दयध्यैmandayadhyai |

उद्वाव्र्षाणोudvāvṛṣāṇo राधसेrādhase तुविष्मानtuviṣmān करनkaran नna इन्द्रःindraḥ सुतीर्थाभयंsutīrthābhayaṃ चca ||
Make his cars hear, that he may show his vigour and may be joyful in the way he loveth.
May mighty Indra pouring forth in bounty bestow on us good roads and perfect safety;
4.29.3
अछाachā योyo गन्ताghantā नाधमानमnādhamānam ऊतीūtī इत्थाitthā विप्रंvipraṃ हवमानंhavamānaṃ गर्णन्तमghṛṇantam |

उपupa तमनिtmani दधानोdadhāno धुर्यdhury आशूनāśūn सहस्राणिsahasrāṇi शतानिśatāni वज्रबाहुःvajrabāhuḥ ||
He who with succour comes to his implorer, the singer here who with his song invites him;
He who himself sets to the pole swift Coursers, he who hath hundreds, thousands, Thunder-wielder.
4.29.4
तवोतासोtvotāso मघवन्नmaghavann इन्द्रindra विप्राviprā वयंvayaṃ तेte सयामsyāma सूरयोsūrayo गर्णन्तःghṛṇantaḥ |

भेजानासोbhejānāso बर्हद्दिवस्यbṛhaddivasya रायrāya आकाय्यस्यākāyyasya दावनेdāvane पुरुक्षोःpurukṣoḥ ||
O Indra Maghavan, by thee protected may we be thine, princes and priests and singers,
Sharing the riches sent from lofty heaven which yields much food, and all desire its bounty.
