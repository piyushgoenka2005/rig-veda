HYMN LXXII. MITRA-VARUṆA.

आā मित्रेmitre वरुणेvaruṇe वयंvayaṃ गीर्भिरghīrbhir जुहुमोjuhumo अत्रिवतatrivat |

निni बर्हिषिbarhiṣi सदतंsadataṃ सोमपीतयेsomapītaye ||
To Varuṇa and Mitra we offer with songs, as Atri did. Sit on the sacred grass to drink the Soma juice.
5.72.1
वरतेनvratena सथोstho धरुवक्षेमाdhruvakṣemā धर्मणाdharmaṇā यातयज्जनाyātayajjanā |

निni बर्हिषिbarhiṣi सदतंsadataṃ सोमपीतयेsomapītaye ||
By Ordinance and Law ye dwell in peace secure, bestirring men.
Sit on the sacred grass to drink the Soma juice.
5.72.2
मित्रशmitraś चca नोno वरुणशvaruṇaś चca जुषेतांjuṣetāṃ यज्ञमyajñam इष्टयेiṣṭaye |

निni बर्हिषिbarhiṣi सदतांsadatāṃ सोमपीतयेsomapītaye ||
May Varuṇa and Mitra, for our help, accept the sacrifice.
Sit on the sacred grass to drink the Soma juice.
5.72.3
