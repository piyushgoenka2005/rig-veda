HYMN LXXVII. AŚVINS.

परातर्यावाणाprātaryāvāṇā परथमाprathamā यजध्वमyajadhvam पुराpurā गर्ध्रादghṛdhrād अररुषःararuṣaḥ पिबातःpibātaḥ |

परातरprātar हिhi यज्ञमyajñam अश्विनाaśvinā दधातेdadhāte परpra शंसन्तिśaṃsanti कवयःkavayaḥ पूर्वभाजःpūrvabhājaḥ ||
FIRST worship those who come at early morning: let the Twain drink before the giftless niggard.
The Aśvins claim the sacrifice at daybreak: the sages yielding the first share extol them.
5.77.1
परातरprātar यजध्वमyajadhvam अश्विनाaśvinā हिनोतhinota नna सायमsāyam अस्तिasti देवयाdevayā अजुष्टमajuṣṭam |

उतान्योutānyo अस्मदasmad यजतेyajate विvi चावःcāvaḥ पूर्वः-पूर्वोpūrvaḥ-pūrvo यजमानोyajamāno वनीयानvanīyān ||
Worship at dawn and instigate the Aśvins: nor is the worshipper at eve rejected.
Besides ourselves another craves and worships: each first in worship is most highly favoured.
5.77.2
हिरण्यत्वङhiraṇyatvaṅ मधुवर्णोmadhuvarṇo घर्तस्नुःghṛtasnuḥ पर्क्षोpṛkṣo वहन्नvahann आā रथोratho वर्ततेvartate वामvām |

मनोजवाmanojavā अश्विनाaśvinā वातरंहाvātaraṃhā येनातियाथोyenātiyātho दुरितानिduritāni विश्वाviśvā ||
Covered with gold, meath-tinted, dropping fatness, your chariot with its freight of food comes hither,
Swift as thought, Aśvins, rapid as the tempest, wherewith ye travel over all obstructions.
5.77.3
योyo भूयिष्ठंbhūyiṣṭhaṃ नासत्याभ्यांnāsatyābhyāṃ विवेषviveṣa चनिष्ठमcaniṣṭham पित्वोpitvo ररतेrarate विभागेvibhāghe |

सsa तोकमtokam अस्यasya पीपरचpīparac छमीभिरchamībhir अनूर्ध्वभासःanūrdhvabhāsaḥ सदमsadam इतit तुतुर्यातtuturyāt ||
He who hath served most often the Nāsatyas, and gives the sweetest food at distribution,
Furthers with his own holy works his offspring, and ever passes those whose flames ascend not.
5.77.4
समsam अश्विनोरaśvinor अवसाavasā नूतनेनnūtanena मयोभुवाmayobhuvā सुप्रणीतीsupraṇītī गमेमghamema |

आā नोno रयिंrayiṃ वहतमvahatam ओतota वीरानvīrān आā विश्वान्यviśvāny अम्र्ताamṛtā सौभगानिsaubhaghāni ||
May we obtain the Aśvins' newest favour, and gain their health-bestowing happy guidance.
Bring riches hither unto us, and heroes, and all felicity and joy, Immortals!
