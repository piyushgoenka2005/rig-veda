HYMN LXV. MITRA-VARUṆA.

परतिprati वांvāṃ सूरsūra उदितेudite सूक्तैर्मित्रंsūktairmitraṃ हुवेhuve वरुणंvaruṇaṃ पूतदक्षमpūtadakṣam |

ययोरसुर्यमक्षितंyayorasuryamakṣitaṃ जयेष्ठंjyeṣṭhaṃ विश्वस्यviśvasya यामन्नाचिताyāmannācitā जिगत्नुjighatnu ||
WITH hymns I call you, when the Sun hath risen, Mitra, and Varuṇa whose thoughts are holy,
Whose Power Divine, supreme and everlasting, comes with good heed at each man's supplication.
7.65.1
ताtā हिhi देवानामसुराdevānāmasurā तावर्याtāvaryā ताtā नःnaḥ कषितीःkṣitīḥ करतमूर्जयन्तीःkaratamūrjayantīḥ |

अश्यामaśyāma मित्रावरुणाmitrāvaruṇā वयंvayaṃ वांvāṃ दयावाdyāvā चca यत्रyatra पीपयन्नहाpīpayannahā चca ||
For they are Asuras of Gods, the friendly make, both of you, our lands exceeding fruitful.
May we obtain you, Varuṇa and Mitra, wherever Heaven and Earth and days may bless us.
7.65.2
ताtā भूरिपाशावन्र्तस्यbhūripāśāvanṛtasya सेतूsetū दुरत्येतूduratyetū रिपवेripave मर्त्यायmartyāya |

रतस्यṛtasya मित्रावरुणाmitrāvaruṇā पथाpathā वामपोvāmapo नna नावाnāvā दुरिताduritā तरेमtarema ||
Bonds of the sinner, they bear many nooses: the wicked mortal hardly may escape them.
Varuṇa-Mitra, may your path of Order bear us o’er trouble as a boat o’er waters.
7.65.3
आā नोno मित्रावरुणाmitrāvaruṇā हव्यजुष्टिंhavyajuṣṭiṃ घर्तैर्गव्यूतिमुक्षतमिळाभिःghṛtairghavyūtimukṣatamiḷābhiḥ |

परतिprati वामत्रvāmatra वरमाvaramā जनायjanāya पर्णीतमुद्नोpṛṇītamudno दिव्यस्यdivyasya चारोःcāroḥ ||
Come, taste our offering, Varuṇa and Mitra: bedew our pasture wil sweet food and fatness.
Pour down in plenty here upon the people the choicest of your fair celestial water.
7.65.4
एषeṣa सतोमोstomo वरुणvaruṇa मित्रmitra ...... ||
To you this laud, O Varuṇa and Mitra, is offered, like bright Soma juice to Vāyu.
Favour our songs of praise, wake thought and spirit. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.
