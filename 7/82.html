HYMN LXXXIT. INDRA-VARUṆA

इन्द्रावरुणाindrāvaruṇā युवमध्वरायyuvamadhvarāya नोno विशेviśe जनायjanāya महिmahi शर्मśarma यछतमyachatam |

दीर्घप्रयज्युमतिdīrghaprayajyumati योyo वनुष्यतिvanuṣyati वयंvayaṃ जयेमjayema पर्तनासुpṛtanāsu दूढ्यःdūḍhyaḥ ||
GRANT us your strong protection, IndraVaruṇa, our people, and our family, for sacrifice.
May we subdue in fight our evil-hearted foes, him who attacks the man steadfast in lengthened rites.
7.82.1
सम्राळsamrāḷ अन्यःanyaḥ सवराळsvarāḷ अन्यanya उच्यतेucyate वांvāṃ महान्ताविन्द्रावरुणाmahāntāvindrāvaruṇā महावसूmahāvasū |

विश्वेviśve देवासःdevāsaḥ परमेparame वयोमनिvyomani संsaṃ वामोजोव्र्षणाvāmojovṛṣaṇā संsaṃ बलंbalaṃ दधुःdadhuḥ ||
O Indra-Varuṇa, mighty and very rich One of you is called Monarch and One Autocrat.
All Gods in the most lofty region of the air have, O ye Steers, combined all power and might in you.
7.82.2
अन्वपांanvapāṃ खान्यत्र्न्तमोजसाkhānyatṛntamojasā सूर्यमैरयतंsūryamairayataṃ दिविdivi परभुprabhu ||
Ye with your strength have pierced the fountains of the floods: the Sun have ye brought forward as the Lord in heaven.
Cheered by this magic draught ye, Indra-Varuṇa, made the dry places stream, made songs of praise flow forth.
7.82.3
मm |

इन्द्रावरुणाindrāvaruṇā मदेmade अस्यasya मायिनो.अपिन्वतमपितःmāyino.apinvatamapitaḥ पिन्वतंpinvataṃ धियःdhiyaḥ ||
In battels and in frays we ministering priests, kneeling upon our knees for furtherance of our weal,
Invoke you, only you, the Lords of twofold wealth, you prompt to hear, we bards, O Indra-Varuṇa.
7.82.4
युवामिदyuvāmid युत्सुyutsu पर्तनासुpṛtanāsu वह्नयोvahnayo युवांyuvāṃ कषेमस्यkṣemasya परसवेprasave मितज्ञवःmitajñavaḥ |

ईशानाīśānā वस्वvasva उभयस्यubhayasya कारवkārava इन्द्रावरुणाindrāvaruṇā सुहवाsuhavā हवामहेhavāmahe ||
O Indra-Varuṇa, as ye created all these creatures of the world by your surpassing might,
In peace and quiet Mitra waits on Varuṇa, the Other, awful, with the Maruis seeks renown.
7.82.5
इन्द्रावरुणाindrāvaruṇā यदिमानिyadimāni चक्रथुर्विश्वाcakrathurviśvā जातानिjātāni भुवनस्यमज्मनाbhuvanasyamajmanā |

कषेमेणkṣemeṇa मित्रोmitro वरुणंvaruṇaṃ दुवस्यतिduvasyati मरुद्भिरुग्रःmarudbhirughraḥ शुभमन्यśubhamanya ईयतेīyate ||
That Varuṇa's high worth may shine preeminent, these Twain have measured each his proper power and might.
The One subdueth the destructive enemy; the Other with a few furthereth many a man.
7.82.6
महेmahe शुल्कायśulkāya वरुणस्यvaruṇasya नुnu तविषtviṣa ओजोojo मिमातेmimāte धरुवमस्यdhruvamasya यतyat सवमsvam |

अजामिमन्यःajāmimanyaḥ शनथयन्तमातिरदśnathayantamātirad दभ्रेभिरन्यःdabhrebhiranyaḥ परpra वर्णोतिvṛṇoti भूयसःbhūyasaḥ ||
No trouble, no misfortune, Indra-Varuṇa, no woe from any side assails the mortal man
Whose sacrifice, O Gods, ye visit and enjoy: ne’er doth the crafty guile of mortal injure him.
7.82.7
नna तमंहोtamaṃho नna दुरितानिduritāni मर्त्यमिन्द्रावरुणाmartyamindrāvaruṇā नna तपःtapaḥ कुतश्चनkutaścana |

यस्यyasya देवाdevā गछथोghachatho वीथोvītho अध्वरंadhvaraṃ नna तंtaṃ मर्तस्यmartasya नशतेnaśate परिह्व्र्तिःparihvṛtiḥ ||
With your divine protection, Heroes, come to us: mine invncation hear, if ye be pleased therewith.
Bestow ye upon us, O Indra-Varuṇa, your friendship and your kinship and your favouring grace.
7.82.8
अर्वांarvāṃ नराnarā दैव्येनावसाdaivyenāvasā गतंghataṃ शर्णुतंśṛṇutaṃ हवंhavaṃ यदिyadi मेme जुजोषथःjujoṣathaḥ |

युवोर्हिyuvorhi सख्यमुतsakhyamuta वाvā यदाप्यंyadāpyaṃ मार्डीकमिन्द्रावरुणाmārḍīkamindrāvaruṇā निni यछतमyachatam ||
In battle after battle, Indra-Varuṇa, be ye our Champions, ye who are the peoples' strength,
When both opposing bands invoke you for the fight, and men that they may gain offspring and progeny.
7.82.9
अस्माकमिन्द्रावरुणाasmākamindrāvaruṇā भरे-भरेbhare-bhare पुरोयोधाpuroyodhā भवतंbhavataṃ कर्ष्ट्योजसाkṛṣṭyojasā |

यदyad वांvāṃ हवन्तhavanta उभयेubhaye अधadha सप्र्धिspṛdhi नरस्तोकस्यnarastokasya तनयस्यtanayasya सातिषुsātiṣu ||
May Indra, Varuṇa, Mitra, and Aryaman vouchsafe us glory and great shelter spreading far.
We think of the beneficent light of Aditi, and Savitar's song of praise, the God who strengthens Law.
7.82.10
अस्मेasme इन्द्रोindro वरुणोvaruṇo मित्रोmitro अर्यमाaryamā दयुम्नंdyumnaṃ यछन्तुyachantu महिmahi शर्मसप्रथःśarmasaprathaḥ |

अवध्रंavadhraṃ जयोतिरदितेरताव्र्धोjyotiraditertāvṛdho देवस्यdevasya शलोकंślokaṃ सवितुर्मनामहेsaviturmanāmahe ||
