HYMN LXIII. MITRA-VARUṆA.

उदud वेतिveti सुभगोsubhagho विश्वचक्षाःviśvacakṣāḥ साधारणःsādhāraṇaḥ सूर्योsūryo मानुषाणामmānuṣāṇām |

चक्षुर्मित्रस्यcakṣurmitrasya वरुणस्यvaruṇasya देवश्चर्मेवdevaścarmeva यःyaḥ समविव्यकsamavivyak तमांसिtamāṃsi ||
COMMON to all mankind, auspicious Sūrya, he who beholdeth all, is mounting upward;
The God, the eye of Varuṇa and Mitra, who rolled up darkness like a piece of leather.
7.63.1
उदud वेतिveti परसवीताprasavītā जनानांjanānāṃ महानmahān केतुरर्णवःketurarṇavaḥ सूर्यस्यsūryasya |

समानंsamānaṃ चक्रंcakraṃ पर्याविव्र्त्सनparyāvivṛtsan यदेतशोyadetaśo वहतिvahati धूर्षुdhūrṣu युक्तःyuktaḥ ||
Sūrya's great ensign, restless as the billow, that urgeth men to action, is advancing:
Onward he still would roll the wheel well-rounded, which Etaśa, harnessed to the car-pole, moveth.
7.63.2
विभ्राजमानvibhrājamāna उषसामुपस्थादuṣasāmupasthād रेभैरुदेत्यनुमद्यमानःrebhairudetyanumadyamānaḥ |

एषeṣa मेme देवःdevaḥ सविताsavitā चछन्दcachanda यःyaḥ समानंsamānaṃ नna परमिनातिधामpraminātidhāma ||
Refulgent from the bosom of the Mornings, he in Whom singers take delight ascendeth.
This Savitar, God, is my chief joy and pleasure, who breaketh not the universal statute.
7.63.3
दिवोdivo रुक्मrukma उरुचक्षाurucakṣā उदेतिudeti दूरेर्थस्तरणिर्भ्राजमानःdūrearthastaraṇirbhrājamānaḥ |

नूनंnūnaṃ जनाःjanāḥ सूर्येणsūryeṇa परसूताprasūtā अयन्नर्थानिayannarthāni कर्णवन्नपांसिkṛṇavannapāṃsi ||
Golden, far-seeing, from the heaven he riseth: far is his goal, he hasteth on resplendent.
Men, verily, inspirited by Sūrya speed to their aims and do the work assigned them.
7.63.4
यत्राyatrā चक्रुरम्र्ताcakruramṛtā गातुमस्मैghātumasmai शयेनोśyeno नna दीयन्नन्वेतिdīyannanveti पाथःpāthaḥ |

परतिprati वांvāṃ सूरsūra उदितेudite विधेमvidhema नमोभिर्मित्रावरुणोतnamobhirmitrāvaruṇota हव्यैःhavyaiḥ ||
Where the irrunortals have prepared his pathway he flieth through the region like a falcon.
With homage and oblations will we serve you, O Mitra-Varuṇa, when the Sun hath risen.
7.63.5
नूnū मित्रोmitro वरुणोvaruṇo अर्यमाaryamā ...... ||
Now Mitra, Varuṇa, Aryaman vouchsafe us freedom and room, for us and for our children.
May we find paths all fair and good to travel. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.
7.63.6
