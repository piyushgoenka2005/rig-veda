HYMN LX. SOMA PAVAMANA.

परpra गायत्रेणghāyatreṇa गायतghāyata पवमानंpavamānaṃ विचर्षणिमvicarṣaṇim |

इन्दुंinduṃ सहस्रचक्षसमsahasracakṣasam ||
SING forth and laud with sacred song most active Pavamana, laud
Indu who sees with thousand eyes.
9.60.1
तंtaṃ तवाtvā सहस्रचक्षसमथोsahasracakṣasamatho सहस्रभर्णसमsahasrabharṇasam |

अतिati वारमपाविषुःvāramapāviṣuḥ ||
Thee who hast thousand eyes to see, bearer of thousand burthens, they
Have filtered through the fleecy cloth.
9.60.2
अतिati वारानvārān पवमानोpavamāno असिष्यदतasiṣyadat कलशानभिkalaśānabhi धावतिdhāvati |

इन्द्रस्यindrasya हार्द्याविशनhārdyāviśan ||
He, Pavamana, hath streamed through the fleece then: he runs into the jars,
Finding his way to Indra's heart.
9.60.3
इन्द्रस्यindrasya सोमsoma राधसेrādhase शंśaṃ पवस्वpavasva विचर्षणेvicarṣaṇe |

परजावदprajāvad रेताretaā भरbhara ||
That Indra may be bounteous, flow, most active Soma, for our weal:
Bring genial seed with progeny.
9.60.4
