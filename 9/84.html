HYMN LXXXIV. SOMA PAVAMANA.

पवस्वpavasva देवमादनोdevamādano विचर्षणिरप्साvicarṣaṇirapsā इन्द्रायindrāya वरुणायvaruṇāya वायवेvāyave |

कर्धीkṛdhī नोno अद्यadya वरिवःvarivaḥ सवस्तिमदुरुक्षितौsvastimadurukṣitau गर्णीहिghṛṇīhi दैव्यंdaivyaṃ जनमjanam ||
FLOW, cheering Gods, most active, winner of the flood, for Indra, and for Vāyu, and for Varuṇa.
Bestow on us to-day wide room with happiness, and in tbine ample dwelling laud the Host of Heaven.
9.84.1
आā यस्तस्थौyastasthau भुवनान्यमर्त्योbhuvanānyamartyo विश्वानिviśvāni सोमःsomaḥ परिpari तान्यर्षतिtānyarṣati |

कर्ण्वनkṛṇvan संच्र्तंsaṃcṛtaṃ विच्र्तमभिष्टयvicṛtamabhiṣṭaya इन्दुःinduḥ सिषक्त्युषसंsiṣaktyuṣasaṃ नna सूर्यःsūryaḥ ||
He who hath come anear to creatures that have life, Immortal Soma flows onward to all of them.
Effecting, for our aid, both union and release, Indu, like Sūrya, follows closely after Dawn.
9.84.2
आā योyo गोभिःghobhiḥ सर्ज्यतsṛjyata ओषधीष्वाoṣadhīṣvā देवानांdevānāṃ सुम्नsumna इषयन्नुपावसुःiṣayannupāvasuḥ |

आā विद्युताvidyutā पवतेpavate धारयाdhārayā सुतsuta इन्द्रंindraṃ सोमोsomo मादयनmādayan दैव्यंdaivyaṃ जनमjanam ||
He who is poured with milk, he who within the plants hastes bringing treasure for the happiness of Gods,
He, poured forth in a stream flows with the lightning's flash, Soma who gladdens Indra and the Host of Heaven.
9.84.3
एषeṣa सयsya सोमःsomaḥ पवतेpavate सहस्रजिदsahasrajid धिन्वानोdhinvāno वाचमिषिरामुषर्बुधमvācamiṣirāmuṣarbudham |

इन्दुःinduḥ समुद्रमुदियर्तिsamudramudiyarti वायुभिरेन्द्रस्यvāyubhirendrasya हार्दिकलशेषुhārdikalaśeṣu सीदतिsīdati ||
Winner of thousands, he, this Soma, flows along, raising a vigorous voice that wakens with the dawn.
Indu with winds drives on the ocean of the air, he sinks within the jars, he rests in Indra's heart.
9.84.4
अभिabhi तयंtyaṃ गावःghāvaḥ पयसाpayasā पयोव्र्धंpayovṛdhaṃ सोमंsomaṃ शरीणन्तिśrīṇanti मतिभिःmatibhiḥ सवर्विदमsvarvidam |

धनंजयःdhanaṃjayaḥ पवतेpavate कर्त्व्योkṛtvyo रसोraso विप्रःvipraḥ कविःकाव्येनाkaviḥkāvyenā सवर्चनाःsvarcanāḥ ||
The kine with milk dress him who makes the milk increase, Soma, amid the songs, who finds the light of heaven.
Winner of wealth, the effectual juice is flowing on, Singer and Sage by wisdom, dear as heaven itself.
9.84.5
