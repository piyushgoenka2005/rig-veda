HYMN XCVII. SOMA PAVAMANA

अस्यasya परेषाpreṣā हेमनाhemanā पूयमानोpūyamāno देवोdevo देवेभिःdevebhiḥ समप्र्क्तsamapṛkta रसमrasam |

सुतःsutaḥ पवित्रंpavitraṃ पर्येतिparyeti रेभनrebhan मितेवmiteva सद्मsadma पशुमान्तिpaśumānti होताhotā ||
MADE pure by this man's urgent zeal and impulse the God hath to the Gods his juice imparted.
He goes, effused and singing, to the filter, like priest to measured seats supplied with cattle.
9.97.1
भद्राbhadrā वस्त्राvastrā समन्याsamanyā वसानोvasāno महानmahān कविर्निवचनानिkavirnivacanāni शंसनśaṃsan |

आā वच्यस्वvacyasva चम्वोःcamvoḥ पूयमानोpūyamāno विचक्षणोvicakṣaṇo जाग्र्विर्देववीतौjāghṛvirdevavītau ||
Robed in fair raiment meet to wear in battle, a mighty Sage pronouncing invocations.
Roll onward to the beakers as they cleanse thee, far-seeing at the feast of Gods, and watchful.
9.97.2
समुsamu परियोpriyo मर्ज्यतेmṛjyate सानोsāno अव्येavye यशस्तरोyaśastaro यशसांyaśasāṃ कषैतोkṣaito अस्मेasme |

अभिabhi सवरsvara धन्वाdhanvā पूयमानोpūyamāno यूयंyūyaṃ पातpāta सवस्तिभिःsvastibhiḥ सदाsadā नःnaḥ ||
Dear, he is brightened on the fleecy summit, a Prince among us, nobler than the noble.
Roar out as thou art purified, run forward. Do ye preserve us evermore with blessings.
9.97.3
परpra गायताभ्यर्चामghāyatābhyarcāma देवानdevān सोमंsomaṃ हिनोतhinota महतेmahate धनायdhanāya |

सवादुःsvāduḥ पवातेpavāte अतिati वारमव्यमाvāramavyamā सीदातिsīdāti कलशंkalaśaṃ देवयुर्नःdevayurnaḥ ||
Let us sing praises to the Gods: sing loudly, send ye the Soma forth for mighty riches.
Let him flow, sweetly-flavoured, through the filter, and let our pious one rest in the pitcher.
9.97.4
इन्दुर्देवानामुपindurdevānāmupa सख्यमायनsakhyamāyan सहस्रधारःsahasradhāraḥ पवतेpavate मदायmadāya |

नर्भिःnṛbhiḥ सतवानोstavāno अनुanu धामdhāma पूर्वमगन्निन्द्रंpūrvamaghannindraṃ महतेmahate सौभगायsaubhaghāya ||
Winning the friendship of the Deities, Indu flows in a thousand streams to make them joyful.
Praised by the men after the ancient statute, he hath come nigh, for our great bliss, to Indra.
9.97.5
सतोत्रेstotre रायेrāye हरिरर्षाharirarṣā पुनानpunāna इन्द्रंindraṃ मदोmado गछतुghachatu तेte भरायbharāya |

देवैर्याहिdevairyāhi सरथंsarathaṃ राधोrādho अछाachā यूयंyūyaṃ पातpāta सवस्तिभिःsvastibhiḥ सदाsadā नःnaḥ ||
Flow, Gold-hued, cleansing thee, to enrich the singer: let thy juice go to Indra to support him.
Come nigh, together with the Gods, for bounty. Do ye preserve us evermore with blessings.
9.97.6
परpra काव्यमुशनेवkāvyamuśaneva बरुवाणोbruvāṇo देवोdevo देवानांdevānāṃ जनिमाjanimā विवक्तिvivakti |

महिव्रतःmahivrataḥ शुचिबन्धुःśucibandhuḥ पावकःpāvakaḥ पदाpadā वराहोvarāho अभ्येतिabhyeti रेभनrebhan ||
The God declares the Deities' generations, like Uśanā, proclaiming lofty wisdom.
With brilliant kin, far-ruling, sanctifying, the Boar advances, singing, to the places.
9.97.7
परpra हंसासस्त्र्पलंhaṃsāsastṛpalaṃ मन्युमछामादस्तंmanyumachāmādastaṃ वर्षगणाvṛṣaghaṇā अयासुःayāsuḥ |

आङगूष्यंāṅghūṣyaṃ पवमानंpavamānaṃ सखायोsakhāyo दुर्मर्षंdurmarṣaṃ साकंsākaṃ परवदन्तिpravadanti वाणमvāṇam ||
The Swans, the Vrsaganas from anear us have brought their restless spirit to our dwelling.
Friends come to Pavamana meet for praises, and sound in concert their resistless music.
9.97.8
सsa रंहतraṃhata उरुगायस्यurughāyasya जूतिंjūtiṃ वर्थाvṛthā करीळन्तंkrīḷantaṃ मिमतेmimate नna गावःghāvaḥ |

परीणसंparīṇasaṃ कर्णुतेkṛṇute तिग्मश्र्ङगोtighmaśṛṅgho दिवाdivā हरिर्दद्र्शेharirdadṛśe नक्तंnaktaṃ रज्रःṛjraḥ ||
He follows the Wide-strider's rapid movement: cows low, as ’twere, to him who sports at pleasure.
He with the sharpened horns brings forth abundance: the Silvery shines by night, by day the Golden.
9.97.9
इन्दुर्वाजीindurvājī पवतेpavate गोन्योघाghonyoghā इन्द्रेindre सोमःsomaḥ सहsaha इन्वनinvan मदायmadāya |

हन्तिhanti रक्षोrakṣo बाधतेbādhate पर्यरातीर्वरिवःparyarātīrvarivaḥ कर्ण्वनkṛṇvan वर्जनस्यvṛjanasya राजाrājā ||
Strong Indu, bathed in milk, flows on for Indra, Soma exciting strength, to make him joyful.
He quells malignities and slays the demons, the King of mighty power who brings us comfort.
9.97.10
अधadha धारयाdhārayā मध्वाmadhvā पर्चानस्तिरोpṛcānastiro रोमroma पवतेpavate अद्रिदुग्धःadridughdhaḥ |

इन्दुरिन्द्रस्यindurindrasya सख्यंsakhyaṃ जुषाणोjuṣāṇo देवोdevo देवस्यdevasya मत्सरोmatsaro मदायmadāya ||
Then in a stream he flows, milked out with press-stones, mingled with sweetness, through the fleecy filter-
Indu rejoicing in the love of Indra, the God who gladdens, for the God's enjoyment.
9.97.11
अभिabhi परियाणिpriyāṇi पवतेpavate पुनानोpunāno देवोdevo देवानdevān सवेनsvena रसेनrasena पर्ञ्चनpṛñcan |

इन्दुर्धर्माण्यindurdharmāṇy रतुथाṛtuthā वसानोvasāno दशdaśa कषिपोkṣipo अव्यतavyata सानोsāno अव्येavye ||
As he is purified he pours out treasures, a God bedewing Gods with his own juices.
Indu hath, wearing qualities by seasons, on the raised fleece engaged, the ten swift fingers.
9.97.12
वर्षाvṛṣā शोणोśoṇo अभिकनिक्रददabhikanikradad गाghā नदयन्नेतिnadayanneti पर्थिवीमुतpṛthivīmuta दयामdyām |

इन्द्रस्येवindrasyeva वग्नुराvaghnurā शर्ण्वśṛṇva आजौājau परचेतयन्नर्षतिpracetayannarṣati वाचमेमामvācamemām ||
The Red Bull bellowing to the kine advances, causing the heavens and earth to roar and tbunder.
Well is he beard like Indra's shout in battle: letting this voice be known he hastens hither.
9.97.13
रसाय्यःrasāyyaḥ पयसाpayasā पिन्वमानpinvamāna ईरयन्नेषिīrayanneṣi मधुमन्तमंशुमmadhumantamaṃśum |

पवमानःpavamānaḥ सन्तनिमेषिsantanimeṣi कर्ण्वन्निन्द्रायkṛṇvannindrāya सोमsoma परिषिच्यमानःpariṣicyamānaḥ ||
Swelling with milk, abounding in sweet flavours, urging the meath-rich plant thou goest onward.
Raising a shout thou flowest as they cleanse thee, when thou, O Soma, art effused for Indra.
9.97.14
एवाevā पवस्वpavasva मदिरोmadiro मदायोदग्राभस्यmadāyodaghrābhasya नमयनnamayan वधस्नैःvadhasnaiḥ |

परिpari वर्णंvarṇaṃ भरमाणोbharamāṇo रुशन्तंruśantaṃ गव्युर्नोghavyurno अर्षarṣa परिpari सोमsoma सिक्तःsiktaḥ ||
So flow thou on inspiriting, for rapture, aiming deatb-shafts at him who stays the waters,
Flow to us wearing thy resplendent colour, effused and eager for the kine, O Soma.
9.97.15
जुष्ट्वीjuṣṭvī नna इन्दोindo सुपथाsupathā सुगान्युरौsughānyurau पवस्वpavasva वरिवांसिvarivāṃsi कर्ण्वनkṛṇvan |

घनेवghaneva विष्वगviṣvagh दुरितानिduritāni विघ्नन्नधिvighnannadhi षणुनाṣṇunā धन्वdhanva सानोsāno अव्येavye ||
Pleased with us, Indu, send us as thou flowest good easy paths in ample space and comforts.
Dispelling, as ’twere with a club, misfortunes, run o’er the height, run o’er the fleecy summit.
9.97.16
वर्ष्टिंvṛṣṭiṃ नोno अर्षarṣa दिव्यांdivyāṃ जिगत्नुमिळावतींjighatnumiḷāvatīṃ शंगयींśaṃghayīṃ जीरदानुमjīradānum |

सतुकेवstukeva वीताvītā धन्वाdhanvā विचिन्वनvicinvan बन्धून्रिमानवरानिन्दोbandhūnrimānavarānindo वायूनvāyūn ||
Pour on us rain celestial, quickly streaming, refreshing, fraught with health and ready bounty.
Flow, Indu, send these Winds thy lower kinsmen, setting them free like locks of hair unbraided.
9.97.17
गरन्थिंghranthiṃ नna विvi षयṣya गरथितंghrathitaṃ पुनानpunāna रजुंṛjuṃ चca गातुंghātuṃ वर्जिनंvṛjinaṃ चca सोमsoma |

अत्योatyo नna करदोkrado हरिराharirā सर्जानोsṛjāno मर्योmaryo देवdeva धन्वdhanva पस्त्यावानpastyāvān ||
Part, like a knotted tangle, while they cleanse thee, O Soma, righteous and unrighteous conduct.
Neigh like a tawny courser who is loosened, come like a youth, O God, a house-possessor.
9.97.18
जुष्टोjuṣṭo मदायmadāya देवतातdevatāta इन्दोindo परिpari षणुनाṣṇunā धन्वdhanva सानोsāno अव्येavye |

सहस्रधारःsahasradhāraḥ सुरभिरदब्धःsurabhiradabdhaḥ परिpari सरवsrava वाजसातौvājasātau नर्षह्येnṛṣahye ||
For the God's service, for delight, O Indu, run o’er the height, run o'ver the fleecy summit.
With thousand streams, inviolate, sweet-scented, flow on for gain of strength that conquers heroes.
9.97.19
अरश्मानोaraśmāno ये.अरथाye.arathā अयुक्ताayuktā अत्यासोatyāso नna सस्र्जानासsasṛjānāsa आजौājau |

एतेete शुक्रासोśukrāso धन्वन्तिdhanvanti सोमाsomā देवासस्तानुपdevāsastānupa याताyātā पिबध्यैpibadhyai ||
Without a car, without a rein to guide them, unyoked, like coursers started in the contest,
These brilliant drops of Soma juice run forward. Do ye, O Deities, come nigh to drink them.
9.97.20
एवाevā नna इन्दोindo अभिabhi देववीतिंdevavītiṃ परिpari सरवsrava नभोnabho अर्णश्चमूषुarṇaścamūṣu |

सोमोsomo अस्मभ्यंasmabhyaṃ काम्यंkāmyaṃ बर्हन्तंbṛhantaṃ रयिंrayiṃ ददातुdadātu वीरवन्तमुग्रमvīravantamughram ||
So for our banquet of the Gods, O Indu, pour down the rain of heaven into the vessels.
May Soma grant us riches sought with longing, mighty, exceeding strong, with store of heroes.
9.97.21
तक्षदtakṣad यदीyadī मनसोmanaso वेनतोvenato वागvāgh जयेष्ठस्यjyeṣṭhasya वाvā धर्मणिdharmaṇi कषोरनीकेkṣoranīke |

आदीमायनādīmāyan वरमाvaramā वावशानाvāvaśānā जुष्टंjuṣṭaṃ पतिंpatiṃ कलशेkalaśe गावghāva इन्दुमindum ||
What time the loving spirit's word had formed him Chief of all food, by statute of the Highest,
Then loudly lowing came the cows to Indu, the chosen, well-loved Master in the beaker.
9.97.22
परpra दानुदोdānudo दिव्योdivyo दानुपिन्वdānupinva रतंṛtaṃ रतायṛtāya पवतेpavate सुमेधाःsumedhāḥ |

धर्माdharmā भुवदbhuvad वर्जन्यस्यvṛjanyasya राजाrājā परpra रश्मिभिर्दशभिर्भारिraśmibhirdaśabhirbhāri भूमbhūma ||
The Sage, Celestial, liberal, raining bounties, pours as he flows the Genuine for the Truthful.
The King shall be effectual strength's upholder: he by the ten bright reins is mostly guided.
9.97.23
पवित्रेभिःpavitrebhiḥ पवमानोpavamāno नर्चक्षाnṛcakṣā राजाrājā देवानामुतdevānāmuta मर्त्यानामmartyānām |

दविताdvitā भुवदbhuvad रयिपतीrayipatī रयीणांrayīṇāṃ रतंṛtaṃ भरतbharat सुभ्र्तंsubhṛtaṃ चार्विन्दुःcārvinduḥ ||
He who beholds mankind, made pure with filters, the King supreme of Deities and mortals,
From days of old is Treasure-Lord of riches: he, Indu, cherishes fair well-kept Order.
9.97.24
अर्वानिवarvāniva शरवसेśravase सातिमछेन्द्रस्यsātimachendrasya वायोरभिvāyorabhi वीतिमर्षvītimarṣa |

सsa नःnaḥ सहस्राsahasrā बर्हतीरिषोbṛhatīriṣo दाdā भवाbhavā सोमsoma दरविणोवितdraviṇovit पुनानःpunānaḥ ||
Haste, like a steed, to vittory for glory, to Indra's and to Vāyu's entertainment.
Give us food ample, thousandfold: be, Soma, the finder-out of riches when they cleanse thee.
9.97.25
देवाव्योdevāvyo नःnaḥ परिषिच्यमानाःpariṣicyamānāḥ कषयंkṣayaṃ सुवीरंsuvīraṃ धन्वन्तुdhanvantu सोमाःsomāḥ |

आयज्यवःāyajyavaḥ सुमतिंsumatiṃ विश्ववाराviśvavārā होतारोhotāro नna दिवियजोdiviyajo मन्द्रतमाःmandratamāḥ ||
Effused by us let God-delighting Somas bring as they flow a home with noble heroes.
Rich in all boons like priests acquiring favour, the worshippers of heaven, the best of Cheerers.
9.97.26
एवाevā देवdeva देवतातेdevatāte पवस्वpavasva महेmahe सोमsoma पसरसेpsarase देवपानःdevapānaḥ |

महश्चिदmahaścid धिdhi षमसिṣmasi हिताःhitāḥ समर्येsamarye कर्धिkṛdhi सुष्ठानेsuṣṭhāne रोदसीrodasī पुनानःpunānaḥ ||
So, God, for service of the Gods flow onward, flow, drink of Gods, for ample food, O Soma.
For we go forth to war against the mighty make heaven and earth well stablished by thy cleansing.
9.97.27
अश्वोaśvo नोno करदोkrado वर्षभिर्युजानःvṛṣabhiryujānaḥ सिंहोsiṃho नna भीमोbhīmo मनसोmanaso जवीयानjavīyān |

अर्वाचीनैःarvācīnaiḥ पथिभिर्येpathibhirye रजिष्ठाrajiṣṭhā आā पवस्वpavasva सौमनसंsaumanasaṃ नna इन्दोindo ||
Thou, yoked by strong men, neighest like a courser, swifter than thought is, like an awful lion.
By paths directed hitherward, the straightest, send thou us happiness, Indu, while they cleanse thee.
9.97.28
शतंśataṃ धाराdhārā देवजाताdevajātā अस्र्ग्रनasṛghran सहस्रमेनाःsahasramenāḥ कवयोkavayo मर्जन्तिmṛjanti |

इन्दोindo सनित्रंsanitraṃ दिवdiva आā पवस्वpavasva पुरेतासिpuraetāsi महतोmahato धनस्यdhanasya ||
Sprung from the Gods, a hundred streams, a thousand, have been effused: sages prepare and purge them.
Bring us from heaven the means of winnning, Indu; thou art-forerunnner of abundant riches.
9.97.29
दिवोdivo नna सर्गाsarghā असस्र्ग्रमह्नांasasṛghramahnāṃ राजाrājā नna मित्रंmitraṃ परpra मिनातिधीरःminātidhīraḥ |

पितुर्नpiturna पुत्रःputraḥ करतुभिर्यतानkratubhiryatāna आā पवस्वpavasva विशेस्याviśeasyā अजीतिमajītim ||
The streams of days, were poured as ’twere from heaven: the wise King doth not treat his friend unkindly.
Like a son following his father's wishes, grant to this family success and safety.
9.97.30
परpra तेte धाराdhārā मधुमतीरस्र्ग्रनmadhumatīrasṛghran वारानvārān यतyat पूतोpūto अत्येष्यव्यानatyeṣyavyān |

पवमानpavamāna पवसेpavase धामdhāma गोनांghonāṃ जज्ञानःjajñānaḥ सूर्यमपिन्वोsūryamapinvo अर्कैःarkaiḥ ||
Now are thy streams poured forth with all their sweetness, when, purified. thou goest through the filter.
The race of kine is thy gift, Pavarridna: when born thou madest Sūrya rich with brightness.
9.97.31
कनिक्रददनुkanikradadanu पन्थांpanthāṃ रतस्यṛtasya शुक्रोśukro विvi भास्यम्र्तस्यbhāsyamṛtasya धामdhāma |

सsa इन्द्रायindrāya पवसेpavase मत्सरवानmatsaravān हिन्वानोhinvāno वाचंvācaṃ मतिभिःmatibhiḥ कवीनामkavīnām ||
Bright, bellowing aiong the path of Order, thou shinest as the form of life eternal.
Thou flowest on as gladdening drink for Indra, sending thy voice out with the hymns of sages.
9.97.32
दिव्यःdivyaḥ सुपर्णो.अवsuparṇo.ava चक्षिcakṣi सोमsoma पिन्वनpinvan धाराःdhārāḥ कर्मणाkarmaṇā देववीतौdevavītau |

एन्दोendo विशviśa कलशंkalaśaṃ सोमधानंsomadhānaṃ करन्दन्निहिkrandannihi सूर्यस्योपsūryasyopa रश्मिमraśmim ||
Pouring out streams at the Gods’ feast with service, thou, Soma, lookest down, a heavenly Eagle.
Enter the Soma-holding beaker, Indu, and with a roar approach the ray of Sarya.
9.97.33
तिस्रोtisro वाचvāca ईरयतिīrayati परpra वह्निरतस्यvahnirtasya धीतिंdhītiṃ बरह्मणोbrahmaṇo मनीषामmanīṣām |

गावोghāvo यन्तिyanti गोपतिंghopatiṃ पर्छमानाःpṛchamānāḥ सोमंsomaṃ यन्तिyanti मतयोmatayo वावशानाःvāvaśānāḥ ||
Three are the voices that the Courser utters: he speaks the thought of prayer, the law of Order.
To the Cow's Master come the Cows inquiring: the hymns with eager longing come to Soma.
9.97.34
सोमंsomaṃ गावोghāvo धेनवोdhenavo वावशानाःvāvaśānāḥ सोमंsomaṃ विप्राviprā मतिभिःmatibhiḥ पर्छमानाःpṛchamānāḥ |
