HYMN LIII. SOMA PAVAMANA.

उतut तेte शुष्मासोśuṣmāso अस्थूasthū रक्षोrakṣo भिन्दन्तोbhindanto अद्रिवःadrivaḥ |

नुदस्वnudasva याःyāḥ परिस्प्र्धःparispṛdhaḥ ||
O THOU with stones for arms, thy powers, crushing the fiends, have raised themselves:
Chase thou the foes who compass us.
9.53.1
अयाayā निजघ्निरोजसाnijaghnirojasā रथसंगेrathasaṃghe धनेdhane हितेhite |

सतवाstavā अबिभ्युषाabibhyuṣā हर्दाhṛdā ||
Thou conquerest thus with might when car meets car, and when the prize is staked:
With fearless heart will I sing praise.
9.53.2
अस्यasya वरतानिvratāni नाध्र्षेnādhṛṣe पवमानस्यpavamānasya दूढ्याdūḍhyā |

रुजruja यस्त्वाप्र्तन्यतिyastvāpṛtanyati ||
No one with evil thought assails this Pavamana's holy laws:
Crush him who fain would fight with thee.
9.53.3
तंtaṃ हिन्वन्तिhinvanti मदच्युतंmadacyutaṃ हरिंhariṃ नदीषुnadīṣu वाजिनमvājinam |

इन्दुमिन्द्रायindumindrāya मत्सरमmatsaram ||
For Indra to the streams they drive the tawny rapture-dropping Steed,
Indu the bringer of delight.
9.53.4
