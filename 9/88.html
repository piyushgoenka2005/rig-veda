HYMN LXXXVIII. SOMA PAVAMANA.

अयंayaṃ सोमsoma इन्द्रindra तुभ्यंtubhyaṃ सुन्वेsunve तुभ्यंtubhyaṃ पवतेpavate तवमस्यtvamasya पाहिpāhi |

तवंtvaṃ हha यंyaṃ चक्र्षेcakṛṣe तवंtvaṃ वव्र्षvavṛṣa इन्दुंinduṃ मदायmadāya युज्यायyujyāya सोममsomam ||
FOR thee this Soma is effused, O Indra: drink of this juice; for thee the stream is flowing-
Soma, which thou thyself hast made and chosen, even Indu, for thy special drink to cheer thee.
9.88.1
सsa ईंīṃ रथोratho नna भुरिषाळbhuriṣāḷ अयोजिayoji महःmahaḥ पुरूणिpurūṇi सातयेsātaye वसूनिvasūni |

आदींādīṃ विश्वाviśvā नहुष्याणिnahuṣyāṇi जाताjātā सवर्षाताsvarṣātā वनvana ऊर्ध्वाūrdhvā नवन्तnavanta ||
Like a capacious car hath it been harnessed, the Mighty; to acquire abundant treasures.
Then in the sacrifice they celebrated all triumphs won by Nahus -n the battle.
9.88.2
वायुर्नvāyurna योyo नियुत्वानिष्टयामाniyutvāniṣṭayāmā नासत्येवnāsatyeva हवhava आā शम्भविष्ठःśambhaviṣṭhaḥ |

विश्ववारोviśvavāro दरविणोदाdraviṇodā इवiva तमनtman पूषेवpūṣeva धीजवनो.असिdhījavano.asi सोमsoma ||
Like Vāyu with his team, moving at pleasure, most gracious when invoked like both Nāsatyas,
Thou art thyself like the Wealth-Giver, Soma! who grants all boons, like song-inspiring Pūṣan.
9.88.3
इन्द्रोindro नna योyo महाmahā कर्माणिkarmāṇi चक्रिर्हन्ताcakrirhantā वर्त्राणामसिvṛtrāṇāmasi सोमपूर्भितsomapūrbhit |

पैद्वोpaidvo नna हिhi तवमहिनाम्नांtvamahināmnāṃ हन्ताhantā विश्वस्यासिसोमviśvasyāsisoma दस्योःdasyoḥ ||
Like Indra who hath done great deeds, thou, Soma, art slayer of the Vṛtras, Fort-destroyer.
Like Pedu's horse who killed the brood of serpents, thus thou, O Soma, slayest every Dasyu.
9.88.4
अग्निर्नaghnirna योyo वनvana आā सर्ज्यमानोsṛjyamāno वर्थाvṛthā पाजांसिpājāṃsi कर्णुतेkṛṇute नदीषुnadīṣu |

जनोjano नna युध्वाyudhvā महतmahata उपब्दिरियर्तिupabdiriyarti सोमःsomaḥ पवमानूर्मिमpavamānaūrmim ||
Like Agni loosed amid the forest, fiercely he winneth splendour in the running waters.
Like one who fights, the roaring of the mighty, thus Soma Pavamana sends his current.
9.88.5
एतेete सोमाsomā अतिati वाराण्यव्याvārāṇyavyā दिव्याdivyā नna कोशासोkośāso अभ्रवर्षाःabhravarṣāḥ |

वर्थाvṛthā समुद्रंsamudraṃ सिन्धवोsindhavo नna नीचीःnīcīḥ सुतासोsutāso अभिabhi कलशानस्र्ग्रनkalaśānasṛghran ||
These Somas passing through the fleecy filter, like rain descending from the clouds of heaven,
Have been effused and poured into the beakers, swiftly like rivers running lowly seaward.
9.88.6
शुष्मीśuṣmī शर्धोśardho नna मारुतंmārutaṃ पवस्वानभिशस्ताpavasvānabhiśastā दिव्याdivyā यथाविटyathāviṭ |

आपोāpo नna मक्षूmakṣū सुमतिर्भवाsumatirbhavā नःnaḥ सहस्राप्साःsahasrāpsāḥ पर्तनाषाणpṛtanāṣāṇ नna यज्ञःyajñaḥ ||
Flow onward like the potent band of Maruts, like that Celestial Host whom none revileth.
Quickly be gracious unto us like waters, like sacrifice victorious, thousand-fashioned.
9.88.7
राज्ञोrājño नुnu तेte वरुणस्यvaruṇasya वरतानिvratāni ...... ||
Thine are King Varuṇa's eternal statutes, lofty and deep, O Soma, is thy glory.
All-pure art thou like Mitra the beloved, adorable, like Aryaman, O Soma.
9.88.8
