HYMN XL. INDRA.

इन्द्रindra तवाtvā वर्षभंvṛṣabhaṃ वयंvayaṃ सुतेsute सोमेsome हवामहेhavāmahe |

सsa पाहिpāhi मध्वोmadhvo अन्धसःandhasaḥ ||
THEE, Indra, we invoke, the Bull, what time the Soma is expressed.
So drink thou of the savoury juice.
3.40.1
इन्द्रindra करतुविदंkratuvidaṃ सुतंsutaṃ सोमंsomaṃ हर्यharya पुरुष्टुतpuruṣṭuta |

पिबाpibā वर्षस्वvṛṣasva तात्र्पिमtātṛpim ||
Indra, whom many laud, accept the strength-conferring Soma juice:
Quaff, pour down drink that satisfies.
3.40.2
इन्द्रindra परpra णोṇo धितावानंdhitāvānaṃ यज्ञंyajñaṃ विश्वेभिर्देवेभिःviśvebhirdevebhiḥ |

तिरtira सतवानstavāna विश्पतेviśpate ||
Indra, with all the Gods promote our wealth-bestowing sacrifice,
Thou highly-lauded Lord of men.
3.40.3
इन्द्रindra सोमाःsomāḥ सुताsutā इमेime तवtava परpra यन्तिyanti सत्पतेsatpate |

कषयंkṣayaṃ चन्द्रासcandrāsa इन्दवःindavaḥ ||
Lord of the brave, to thee proceed these drops of Soma juice expressed,
The bright drops to thy dwelling-place.
3.40.4
दधिष्वाdadhiṣvā जठरेjaṭhare सुतंsutaṃ सोममिन्द्रsomamindra वरेण्यमvareṇyam |

तवtava दयुक्षासdyukṣāsa इन्दवःindavaḥ ||
Within thy belly, Indra, take juice, Soma the most excellent: Thine are the drops celestial.
3.40.5
गिर्वणःghirvaṇaḥ पाहिpāhi नःnaḥ सुतंsutaṃ मधोर्धाराभिरज्यसेmadhordhārābhirajyase |

इन्द्रindra तवादातमिदtvādātamid यशःyaśaḥ ||
Drink our libation, Lord of hymns: with streams of meath thou art bedewed
Our glory, Indra, is thy gift.
3.40.6
अभिabhi दयुम्नानिdyumnāni वनिनvanina इन्द्रंindraṃ सचन्तेsacante अक्षिताakṣitā |

पीत्वीpītvī सोमस्यsomasya वाव्र्धेvāvṛdhe ||
To Indra go the treasures of the worshipper, which never fail:
He drinks the Soma and is strong
3.40.7
अर्वावतोarvāvato नna आā गहिghahi परावतश्चparāvataśca वर्त्रहनvṛtrahan |

इमाimā जुषस्वjuṣasva नोno गिरःghiraḥ ||
From far away, from near at hand, O Vṛtra-slayer, come to us:
Accept the songs we sing to thee.
3.40.8
यदन्तराyadantarā परावतमर्वावतंparāvatamarvāvataṃ चca हूयसेhūyase |

इन्द्रेहindreha ततtata आā गहिghahi ||
When from the space between the near and far thou art invoked by us,
Thence, Indra. come thou hitherward.
3.40.9
