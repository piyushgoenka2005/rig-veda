HYMN LIX. MITRA.

मित्रोmitro जनानjanān यातयतिyātayati बरुवाणोbruvāṇo मित्रोmitro दाधारdādhāra पर्थिवीमुतpṛthivīmuta दयामdyām |

मित्रःmitraḥ कर्ष्टीरनिमिषाभिkṛṣṭīranimiṣābhi चष्टेcaṣṭe मित्रायmitrāya हव्यंघ्र्तवज्जुहोतhavyaṃghṛtavajjuhota ||
MITRA, when speaking, stirreth men to labour: Mitra sustaineth both the earth and heaven.
Mitra beholdeth men with eyes that close not. To Mitra bring, with holy oil, oblation.
3.59.1
परpra सsa मित्रmitra मर्तोmarto अस्तुastu परयस्वानprayasvān यस्तyasta आदित्यāditya शिक्षतिśikṣati वरतेनvratena |

नna हन्यतेhanyate नna जीयतेjīyate तवोतोtvoto नैनमंहोnainamaṃho अश्नोत्यन्तितोaśnotyantito नna दूरातdūrāt ||
Foremost be he who brings thee food, O Mitra, who strives to keep thy sacred Law, Āditya.
He whom thou helpest ne’er is slain or conquered, on him, from near or far, falls no affliction.
3.59.2
अनमीवासanamīvāsa इळयाiḷayā मदन्तोmadanto मितज्ञवोmitajñavo वरिमन्नाvarimannā पर्थिव्याःpṛthivyāḥ |

आदित्यस्यādityasya वरतमुपक्षियन्तोvratamupakṣiyanto वयंvayaṃ मित्रस्यmitrasya सुमतौsumatau सयामsyāma ||
joying in sacred food and free from sickness, with knees bent lowly on the earth's broad surface,
Following closely the Āditya's statute, may we remain in Mitra's gracious favour.
3.59.3
अयंayaṃ मित्रोmitro नमस्यःnamasyaḥ सुशेवोsuśevo राजाrājā सुक्षत्रोsukṣatro अजनिष्टajaniṣṭa वेधाःvedhāḥ |

तस्यtasya वयंvayaṃ ...... ||
Auspicious and adorable, this Mitra was born with fair dominion, King, Disposer.
May we enjoy the grace of him the Holy, yea, rest in his propitious loving-kindness.
3.59.4
महानादित्योmahānādityo नमसोपसद्योnamasopasadyo यातयज्जनोyātayajjano गर्णतेghṛṇate सुशेवःsuśevaḥ |

तस्माtasmā एततetat पन्यतमायpanyatamāya जुष्टमग्नौjuṣṭamaghnau मित्रायmitrāya हविराhavirā जुहोतjuhota ||
The great Āditya, to be served with wor. ship, who stirreth men, is gracious to the singer.
To Mitra, him most highly to be lauded, offer in fire oblation that he loveth.
3.59.5
मित्रस्यmitrasya चर्षणीध्र्तो.अवोcarṣaṇīdhṛto.avo देवस्यdevasya सानसिsānasi |

दयुम्नंdyumnaṃ चित्रश्रवस्तममcitraśravastamam ||
The gainful grace of Mitra,. God, supporter of the race of man,
Gives splendour of most.glorious fame.
3.59.6
अभिabhi योyo महिनाmahinā दिवंdivaṃ मित्रोmitro बभूवbabhūva सप्रथाःsaprathāḥ |

अभिabhi शरवोभिःśravobhiḥ पर्थिवीमpṛthivīm ||
Mitra whose glory spreads afar, he who in might surpasses heaven,
Surpasses earth in his renown.
3.59.7
मित्रायmitrāya पञ्चpañca येमिरेyemire जनाjanā अभिष्टिशवसेabhiṣṭiśavase |

सsa देवानdevān विश्वानviśvān बिभर्तिbibharti ||
All the Five Races have repaired to Mitra, ever strong to aid,
For he sustaineth all the Gods.
3.59.8
मित्रोmitro देवेष्वायुषुdeveṣvāyuṣu जनायjanāya वर्क्तबर्हिषेvṛktabarhiṣe |

इषiṣa इष्टव्रताकःiṣṭavratāakaḥ ||
Mitra to Gods, to living men, to him who strews the holy grass,
Gives food fulfilling sacred Law.
3.59.9
