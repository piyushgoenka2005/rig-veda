HYMN LI. INDRA.

चर्षणीध्र्तंcarṣaṇīdhṛtaṃ मघवानमुक्थ्यमिन्द्रंmaghavānamukthyamindraṃ गिरोghiro बर्हतीरभ्यनूषतbṛhatīrabhyanūṣata |

वाव्र्धानंvāvṛdhānaṃ पुरुहूतंpuruhūtaṃ सुव्र्क्तिभिरमर्त्यंsuvṛktibhiramartyaṃ जरमाणंjaramāṇaṃ दिवे-दिवेdive-dive ||
HIGH hymns have sounded forth the praise of Maghavan, supporter of mankind, of Indra meet for lauds;
Him who hath waxen great, invoked with beauteous songs, Immortal One, whose praise each day is sung aloud.
3.51.1
शतक्रतुमर्णवंśatakratumarṇavaṃ शाकिनंśākinaṃ नरंnaraṃ गिरोghiro मma इन्द्रमुपindramupa यन्तिyanti विश्वतःviśvataḥ |

वाजसनिंvājasaniṃ पूर्भिदंpūrbhidaṃ तूर्णिमप्तुरंtūrṇimapturaṃ धामसाचमभिषाचंdhāmasācamabhiṣācaṃ सवर्विदमsvarvidam ||
To Indra from all sides go forth my songs of praise, the Lord of Hundred Powers, strong, Hero, like the sea,
Swift, winner of the booty, breaker-down of forts, faithful and ever-glorious, finder of the light.
3.51.2
आकरेākare वसोर्जरिताvasorjaritā पनस्यते.अनेहसpanasyate.anehasa सतुभstubha इन्द्रोindro दुवस्यतिduvasyati |

विवस्वतःvivasvataḥ सदनsadana आā हिhi पिप्रियेpipriye सत्रासाहमभिमातिहनंsatrāsāhamabhimātihanaṃ सतुहिstuhi ||
Where battle's spoil is piled the singer winneth praise, for Indra taketh care of matchless worshippers.
He in Vivasvān's dwelling findeth his delight: praise thou the ever-conquering slayer of the foe.
3.51.3
नर्णामुnṛṇāmu तवाtvā नर्तमंnṛtamaṃ गीर्भिरुक्थैरभिghīrbhirukthairabhi परpra वीरमर्चताvīramarcatā सबाधःsabādhaḥ |

संsaṃ सहसेsahase पुरुमायोpurumāyo जिहीतेjihīte नमोnamo अस्यasya परदिवpradiva एकeka ईशेīśe ||
Thee, valorous, most heroic of the heroes, shall the priests glorify with songg and praises.
Full of all wondrous power he goes to conquest: worship is his, sole Lord from days aforetime.
3.51.4
पूर्वीरस्यpūrvīrasya निष्षिधोniṣṣidho मर्त्येषुmartyeṣu पुरूpurū वसूनिvasūni पर्थिवीpṛthivī बिभर्तिbibharti |

इन्द्रायindrāya दयावdyāva ओषधीरुतापोoṣadhīrutāpo रयिंrayiṃ रक्षन्तिrakṣanti जीरयोjīrayo वनानिvanāni ||
Abundant are the gifts he gives to mortals: for him the earth bears a rich store of treasures.
The heavens, the growing plants, the living waters, the forest trees preserve their wealth for Indra.
3.51.5
तुभ्यंtubhyaṃ बरह्माणिbrahmāṇi गिरghira इन्द्रindra तुभ्यंtubhyaṃ सत्राsatrā दधिरेdadhire हरिवोharivo जुषस्वjuṣasva |

बोध्यापिरवसोbodhyāpiravaso नूतनस्यnūtanasya सखेsakhe वसोvaso जरित्र्भ्योjaritṛbhyo वयोधाःvayodhāḥ ||
To thee, O Indra, Lord of Bays, for ever are offered prayers and songs: accept them gladly.
As Kinsman think thou of some fresh assistance; good Friend, give strength and life to those who praise thee.
3.51.6
इन्द्रindra मरुत्वmarutva इहiha पाहिpāhi सोमंsomaṃ यथाyathā शार्यातेśāryāte अपिबःapibaḥ सुतस्यsutasya |

तवtava परणीतीpraṇītī तवtava शूरśūra शर्मन्नाśarmannā विवासन्तिvivāsanti कवयःसुयज्ञाःkavayaḥsuyajñāḥ ||
Here, Indra, drink thou Soma with the Maruts, as thou didst drink the juice beside Śāryāta.
Under thy guidance, in thy keeping, Hero, the singers serve, skilled in fair sacrifices.
3.51.7
सsa वावशानvāvaśāna इहiha पाहिpāhi सोमंsomaṃ मरुद्भिरिन्द्रmarudbhirindra सखिभिःsakhibhiḥ सुतंsutaṃ नःnaḥ |

जातंjātaṃ यतyat तवाtvā परिpari देवाdevā अभूषनabhūṣan महेmahe भरायbharāya पुरुहूतpuruhūta विश्वेviśve ||
So eagerly desirous drink the Soma, our juice, O Indra, with thy friends the Maruts,
Since at thy birth all Deities adorned thee for the great fight, O thou invoked of many.
3.51.8
अप्तूर्येaptūrye मरुतmaruta आपिरेषो.अमन्दन्निन्द्रमनुāpireṣo.amandannindramanu दातिवाराःdātivārāḥ |

तेभिःtebhiḥ साकंsākaṃ पिबतुpibatu वर्त्रखादःvṛtrakhādaḥ सुतंsutaṃ सोमंsomaṃ दाशुषःdāśuṣaḥ सवेsve सधस्थेsadhasthe ||
He was your comrade in your zeal, O Maruts: they, rich in noble gifts, rejoiced in Indra.
With them together let the Vṛtra-slayer drink in his home the worshipper's libation.
3.51.9
इदंidaṃ हयन्वोजसाhyanvojasā सुतंsutaṃ राधानांrādhānāṃ पतेpate |

पिबाpibā तवस्यtvasya गिर्वणःghirvaṇaḥ ||
So, Lord of affluent gifts, this juice hath been pressed for thee with strength
Drink of it, thou who lovest song.
3.51.10
यस्तेyaste अनुanu सवधामसतsvadhāmasat सुतेsute निni यछyacha तन्वमtanvam |

सsa तवाtvā ममत्तुmamattu सोम्यमsomyam ||
Incline thy body to this juice which suits thy Godlike nature well:
May it cheer thee who lovest it.
3.51.11
परpra तेte अश्नोतुaśnotu कुक्ष्योःkukṣyoḥ परेन्द्रprendra बरह्मणाbrahmaṇā शिरःśiraḥ |

परpra बाहूbāhū शूरśūra राधसेrādhase ||
Brave Indra, let it work through both thy flanks, and through thy head by prayer,
And through thine arms, to prosper us.
3.51.12
