HYMN XXXII. INDRA

इन्द्रindra सोमंsomaṃ सोमपतेsomapate पिबेमंpibemaṃ माध्यन्दिनंmādhyandinaṃ सवनंsavanaṃ चारुcāru यतyat तेte |

परप्रुथ्याprapruthyā शिप्रेśipre मघवन्न्र्जीषिनmaghavannṛjīṣin विमुच्याvimucyā हरीharī इहमादयस्वihamādayasva ||
DRINK thou this Soma, Indra, Lord of Soma; drink thou the draught of noonday which thou Iovest.
Puffing thy cheeks, impetuous, liberal Giver, here loose thy two Bay Horses and rejoice thee.
3.32.1
गवाशिरंghavāśiraṃ मन्थिनमिन्द्रmanthinamindra शुक्रंśukraṃ पिबाpibā सोमंsomaṃ ररिमाrarimā तेte मदायmadāya |

बरह्मक्र्ताbrahmakṛtā मारुतेनाmārutenā गणेनghaṇena सजोषाsajoṣā रुद्रैस्त्र्पदाव्र्षस्वrudraistṛpadāvṛṣasva ||
Quaff it pure, meal-blent, mixt with milk, O Indra; we have poured forth the Soma for thy rapture.
Knit with the prayer-fulfilling band of Maruts, yea, with the Rudras, drink till thou art sated;
3.32.2
येye तेte शुष्मंśuṣmaṃ येye तविषीमवर्धन्नर्चन्तtaviṣīmavardhannarcanta इन्द्रindra मरुतस्तोजःmarutastaojaḥ |

माध्यन्दिनेmādhyandine सवनेsavane वज्रहस्तvajrahasta पिबाpibā रुद्रेभिःrudrebhiḥ सगणःsaghaṇaḥ सुशिप्रsuśipra ||
Those who gave increase to thy strength and vigour; the Maruts singing forth thy might, O Indra.
Drink thou, O fair of cheek, whose hand wields thunder, with Rudras banded, at our noon libation.
3.32.3
तta इनin नवस्यnvasya मधुमदmadhumad विविप्रvivipra इन्द्रस्यindrasya शर्धोśardho मरुतोmaruto यya आसनāsan |

येभिर्व्र्त्रस्येषितोyebhirvṛtrasyeṣito विवेदामर्मणोvivedāmarmaṇo मन्यमानस्यmanyamānasya मर्मmarma ||
They, even the Maruts who were there, excited with song the meath-created strength of Indra.
By them impelled to act he reached the vitals Of Vṛtra, though he deemed that none might wound him.
3.32.4
मनुष्वदिन्द्रmanuṣvadindra सवनंsavanaṃ जुषाणःjuṣāṇaḥ पिबाpibā सोमंsomaṃ शश्वतेśaśvate वीर्यायvīryāya |

सsa आā वव्र्त्स्वvavṛtsva हर्यश्वharyaśva यज्ञैःyajñaiḥ सरण्युभिरपोsaraṇyubhirapo अर्णाarṇā सिसर्षिsisarṣi ||
Pleased, like a man, with our libation, Indra, drink, for enduring hero might, the Soma.
Lord of Bays, moved by sacrifice come hither: thou with the Swift Ones stirrest floods and waters.
3.32.5
तवमपोtvamapo यदyad धdha वर्त्रंvṛtraṃ जघन्वानत्यानिवjaghanvānatyāniva परास्र्जःprāsṛjaḥ सर्तवाजौsartavājau |

शयानमिन्द्रśayānamindra चरतcarata वधेनvadhena वव्रिवांसंvavrivāṃsaṃ परिpari देवीरदेवमdevīradevam ||
When thou didst loose the streams to run like racers in the swift contest, having smitten Vṛtra
With flying weapon where he lay, O Indra, and, godless, kept the Goddesses encompassed.
3.32.6
यजामyajāma इनin नमसाnamasā वर्द्धमिन्द्रंvṛddhamindraṃ बर्हन्तंbṛhantaṃ रष्वमजरंṛṣvamajaraṃ युवानमyuvānam |

यस्यyasya परियेpriye ममतुर्यज्ञियस्यmamaturyajñiyasya नna रोदसीrodasī महिमानंmahimānaṃ ममातेmamāte ||
With reverence let us worship mighty Indra, great and sublime, eternal, everyouthful,
Whose greatness the dear world-halves have not measured, no, nor conceived the might of him the Holy.
3.32.7
इन्द्रस्यindrasya कर्मkarma सुक्र्ताsukṛtā पुरूणिpurūṇi वरतानिvratāni देवाdevā नna मिनन्तिminanti विश्वेviśve |

दाधारdādhāra यःyaḥ पर्थिवींpṛthivīṃ दयामुतेमांdyāmutemāṃ जजानjajāna सूर्यमुषसंsūryamuṣasaṃ सुदंसाःsudaṃsāḥ ||
Many are Indra's nobly wrought achievements, and none of all the Gods transgress his statutes.
He beareth up this earth and heaven, and, doer of marvels, he begot the Sun and Morning.
3.32.8
अद्रोघadrogha सत्यंsatyaṃ तवtava तनtan महित्वंmahitvaṃ सद्योsadyo यज्जातोyajjāto अपिबोapibo हha सोममsomam |

नna दयावdyāva इन्द्रindra तवसस्तtavasasta ओजोojo नाहाnāhā नna मासाःmāsāḥ शरदोśarado वरन्तvaranta ||
Herein, O Guileless One, is thy true greatness, that soon as born thou drankest up the Soma.
Days may not check the power of thee the Mighty, nor the nights, Indra, nor the months, nor autumns.
3.32.9
तवंtvaṃ सद्योsadyo अपिबोapibo जातjāta इन्द्रindra मदायmadāya सोमंsomaṃ परमेparame वयोमनvyoman |

यदyad धdha दयावाप्र्थिवीdyāvāpṛthivī आविवेषीरथाभवःāviveṣīrathābhavaḥ पूर्व्यःpūrvyaḥ कारुधायाःkārudhāyāḥ ||
As soon as thou wast born in highest heaven thou drankest Soma to delight thee, Indra;
And when thou hadst pervaded earth and heaven thou wast the first supporter of the singer.
3.32.10
अहन्नहिंahannahiṃ परिशयानमर्णpariśayānamarṇa ओजायमानंojāyamānaṃ तुविजातtuvijāta तव्यानtavyān |

नna तेte महित्वमनुmahitvamanu भूदधbhūdadha दयौर्यदन्ययाdyauryadanyayā सफिग्याsphighyā कषामवस्थाःkṣāmavasthāḥ ||
Thou, puissant God, more mighty, slewest. Ahi showing his strength when couched around the waters.
The heaven itself attained not to thy greatness when with one hip of thine the earth was shadowed.
3.32.11
यज्ञोyajño हिhi तta इन्द्रindra वर्धनोvardhano भूदुतbhūduta परियःpriyaḥ सुतसोमोsutasomo मियेधःmiyedhaḥ |

यज्ञेनyajñena यज्ञमवyajñamava यज्ञियःyajñiyaḥ सनsan यज्ञस्तेyajñaste वज्रमहिहत्यvajramahihatya आवतāvat ||
Sacrifice, Indra, made thee wax so mighty, the dear oblation with the flowing Soma.
O Worshipful, with worship help our worship, for worship helped thy bolt when slaying Ahi.
3.32.12
यज्ञेनेन्द्रमवसाyajñenendramavasā चक्रेcakre अर्वागैनंarvāghainaṃ सुम्नायsumnāya नव्यसेnavyase वव्र्त्यामvavṛtyām |

यya सतोमेभिर्वाव्र्धेstomebhirvāvṛdhe पूर्व्येभिर्योpūrvyebhiryo मध्यमेभिरुतmadhyamebhiruta नूतनेभिःnūtanebhiḥ ||
With sacrifice and wish have I brought Indra; still for new blessings may I turn him hither,
Him magnified by ancient songs and praises, by lauds of later time and days yet recent.
3.32.13
विवेषviveṣa यनyan माmā धिषणाdhiṣaṇā जजानjajāna सतवैstavai पुराpurā पार्यादिन्द्रमह्नःpāryādindramahnaḥ |

अंहसोaṃhaso यत्रyatra पीपरदpīparad यथाyathā नोno नावेवnāveva यान्तमुभयेyāntamubhaye हवन्तेhavante ||
I have brought forth a song when longing seized me: ere the decisive day will I laud Indra;
Then may he safely bear us over trouble, as in a ship, when both sides invocate him.
3.32.14
आपूर्णोāpūrṇo अस्यasya कलशःkalaśaḥ सवाहाsvāhā सेक्तेवsekteva कोशंkośaṃ सिसिचेsisice पिबध्यैpibadhyai |

समुsamu परियाpriyā आवव्र्त्रनāvavṛtran मदायmadāya परदक्षिणिदभिpradakṣiṇidabhi सोमासैन्द्रमsomāsaindram ||
Full is his chalice: Glory! Like a pourer I have filled up the vessel for his drinking.
Presented on the right, dear Soma juices have brought us Indra, to rejoice him, hither.
3.32.15
नna तवाtvā गभीरःghabhīraḥ पुरुहूतpuruhūta सिन्धुर्नाद्रयःsindhurnādrayaḥ परिpari षन्तोṣanto वरन्तvaranta |

इत्थाitthā सखिभ्यsakhibhya इषितोiṣito यदिन्द्राyadindrā दर्ळ्हंdṛḷhaṃ चिदरुजोcidarujo गव्यमूर्वमghavyamūrvam ||
Not the deep-flowing flood, O Much-invoked One! not hills that compass thee about restrain thee,
Since here incited, for thy friends, O Indra, thou breakest e’en the firm built stall of cattle.
3.32.16
शुनंśunaṃ हुवेमhuvema ...... ||
Call we on Maghavan, auspicious Indra, best Hero in this fight where spoil is gathered,
The Strong who listens, who gives aid in battles, who slays the Vṛtras, wins and gathers riches.
3.32.17
