HYMN XXXIV. INDRA.

इन्द्रःindraḥ पूर्भिदातिरदpūrbhidātirad दासमर्कैर्विदद्वसुर्दयमानोdāsamarkairvidadvasurdayamāno विशत्रूनviśatrūn |

बरह्मजूतस्तन्वाbrahmajūtastanvā वाव्र्धानोvāvṛdhāno भूरिदात्रbhūridātra आप्र्णदāpṛṇad रोदसीrodasī उभेubhe ||
FORT-RENDER, Lord of Wealth, dispelling foemen, Indra with lightnings hath o’ercome the Dāsa.
Impelled by prayer and waxen great in body, he hath filled earth and heaven, the Bounteous Giver.
3.34.1
मखस्यmakhasya तेte तविषस्यtaviṣasya परpra जूतिमियर्मिjūtimiyarmi वाचमम्र्तायvācamamṛtāya भूषनbhūṣan |

इन्द्रindra कषितीनामसिkṣitīnāmasi मानुषीणांmānuṣīṇāṃ विशांviśāṃ दैवीनामुतdaivīnāmuta पूर्वयावाpūrvayāvā ||
I stimulate thy zeal, the Strong, the Hero decking my song of praise forth; Immortal.
O Indra, thou art equally the Leader of heavenly hosts and human generations.
3.34.2
इन्द्रोindro वर्त्रमव्र्णोच्छर्धनीतिःvṛtramavṛṇocchardhanītiḥ परpra मायिनाममिनादmāyināmaminād वर्पणीतिःvarpaṇītiḥ |

अहनahan वयंसमुशधगvyaṃsamuśadhagh वनेष्वाविर्धेनाvaneṣvāvirdhenā अक्र्णोदakṛṇod राम्याणामrāmyāṇām ||
Leading, his band Indra encompassed Vṛtra; weak grew the wily leader of enchanters.
He who burns fierce in forests slaughtered Vyaṁsa, and made the Milch-kine of the nights apparent.
3.34.3
इन्द्रःindraḥ सवर्षाsvarṣā जनयन्नहानिjanayannahāni जिगायोशिग्भिःjighāyośighbhiḥ पर्तनाpṛtanā अभिष्टिःabhiṣṭiḥ |

परारोचयनprārocayan मनवेmanave केतुमह्नामविन्दज्ज्योतिर्ब्र्हतेरणायketumahnāmavindajjyotirbṛhateraṇāya ||
Indra, light-winner, days' Creator, conquered, victorious, hostile bands with those who loved him.
For man the days' bright ensign he illumined, and found the light for his joy and gladness.
3.34.4
इन्द्रस्तुजोindrastujo बर्हणाbarhaṇā आā विवेशviveśa नर्वदnṛvad दधानोdadhāno नर्याnaryā पुरूणिpurūṇi |

अचेतयदacetayad धियdhiya इमाimā जरित्रेjaritre परेमंpremaṃ वर्णमतिरच्छुक्रमासामvarṇamatiracchukramāsām ||
Forward to fiercely falling blows pressed Indra, herolike doing many hero exploits.
These holy songs he taught the bard who gaised him, and widely spread these Dawns' resplendent colour.
3.34.5
महोmaho महानिmahāni पनयन्त्यस्येन्द्रस्यpanayantyasyendrasya कर्मkarma सुक्र्ताsukṛtā पुरूणिpurūṇi |

वर्जनेनvṛjanena वर्जिनानvṛjinān संsaṃ पिपेषpipeṣa मायाभिर्दस्यून्रभिभूत्योजाःmāyābhirdasyūnrabhibhūtyojāḥ ||
They laud the mighty acts of him the Mighty, the many glorious deeds performed by Indra.
He in his strength, with all-surpassing prowess, through wondrous arts crushed the malignant Dasyus.
3.34.6
युधेन्द्रोyudhendro मह्नाmahnā वरिवश्चकारvarivaścakāra देवेभ्यःdevebhyaḥ सत्पतिश्चर्षणिप्राःsatpatiścarṣaṇiprāḥ |

विवस्वतःvivasvataḥ सदनेsadane अस्यasya तानिtāni विप्राviprā उक्थेभिःukthebhiḥ कवयोग्र्णन्तिkavayoghṛṇanti ||
Lord of the brave, Indra who rules the people gave freedom to the Gods by might and battle.
Wise singers glorify with chanted praises these his achievements in Vivasvān's dwelling.
3.34.7
सत्रासाहंsatrāsāhaṃ वरेण्यंvareṇyaṃ सहोदांsahodāṃ ससवांसंsasavāṃsaṃ सवरपश्चsvarapaśca देवीःdevīḥ |

ससानsasāna यःyaḥ पर्थिवींpṛthivīṃ दयामुतेमामिन्द्रंdyāmutemāmindraṃ मदन्त्यनुmadantyanu धीरणासःdhīraṇāsaḥ ||
Excellent, Conqueror, the victory-giver, the winner of the light and Godlike Waters,
He who hath won this broad earth and this heaven, -in Indra they rejoice who love devotions.
3.34.8
ससानात्यानुतsasānātyānuta सूर्यंsūryaṃ ससानेन्द्रःsasānendraḥ ससानsasāna पुरुभोजसंpurubhojasaṃ गामghām |

हिरण्ययमुतhiraṇyayamuta भोगंbhoghaṃ ससानsasāna हत्वीhatvī दस्यूनdasyūn परार्यंवर्णमावतprāryaṃvarṇamāvat ||
He gained possession of the Sun and Horses, Indra obtained the Cow who feedeth many.
Treasure of gold he won; he smote the Dasyus, and gave protection to the Āryan colour.
3.34.9
इन्द्रindra ओषधीरसनोदहानिoṣadhīrasanodahāni वनस्पतीन्रसनोदन्तरिक्षमvanaspatīnrasanodantarikṣam |

बिभेदbibheda वलंvalaṃ नुनुदेnunude विवाचो.अथाभवदvivāco.athābhavad दमितद्भिक्रतूनामdamitadbhikratūnām ||
He took the plants and days for his possession; he gained the forest trees and air's mid-region.
Vala he cleft, and chased away opponents: thus was he tamer of the overweening.
3.34.10
शुनंśunaṃ हुवेमhuvema ...... ||
Call we on Maghavan, auspicious Indra, best Hero in the fight where spoil is gathered,
The Strong, who listens, who gives aid in battles, who slays the Vṛtras, wins and gathers treasures.
