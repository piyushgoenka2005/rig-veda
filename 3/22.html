HYMN XXII. AGNI.

अयंayaṃ सोso अग्निर्यस्मिनaghniryasmin सोममिन्द्रःsomamindraḥ सुतंsutaṃ दधेdadhe जठरेjaṭhare वावशानःvāvaśānaḥ |

सहस्रिणंsahasriṇaṃ वाजमत्यंvājamatyaṃ नna सप्तिंsaptiṃ ससवानsasavān सनsan सतूयसेstūyase जातवेदःjātavedaḥ ||
THIS is that Agni whence the longing Indra took the pressed Soma deep within his body.
Winner of spoils in thousands, like a courser, with praise art thou exalted, Jātavedas.
3.22.1
अग्नेaghne यतyat तेte दिविdivi वर्चःvarcaḥ पर्थिव्यांpṛthivyāṃ यदोषधीष्वप्स्वाyadoṣadhīṣvapsvā यजत्रyajatra |

येनान्तरिक्षमुर्वाततन्थyenāntarikṣamurvātatantha तवेषःtveṣaḥ सsa भानुरर्णवोbhānurarṇavo नर्चक्षाःnṛcakṣāḥ ||
That light of thine in heaven and earth, O Agni, in plants, O Holy One, and in the waters,
Wherewith thou hast spread wide the air's mid-region-bright is that splendour, wavy, man-beholding.
3.22.2
अग्नेaghne दिवोdivo अर्णमछाarṇamachā जिगास्यछाjighāsyachā देवानूचिषेdevānūciṣe धिष्ण्यायेdhiṣṇyāye |

याyā रोचनेrocane परस्तातparastāt सूर्यस्यsūryasya याश्चावस्तादुपतिष्ठण्तyāścāvastādupatiṣṭhaṇta आपःāpaḥ ||
O Agni, to the sea of heaven thou goest: thou hast called hither Gods beheld in spirit.
The waters, too, come hither, those up yonder in the Sun's realm of light, and those beneath it.
3.22.3
पुरीष्यासोpurīṣyāso अग्नयःaghnayaḥ परावणेभिःprāvaṇebhiḥ सजोषसःsajoṣasaḥ |

जुषन्तांjuṣantāṃ यज्ञमद्रुहो.अनमीवाyajñamadruho.anamīvā इषोiṣo महीःmahīḥ ||
Let fires that dwell in mist, combined with those that have their home in floods,
Guileless accept our sacrifice, great viands free from all disease.
3.22.4
इळामग्नेiḷāmaghne ...... ||
Agni, as holy food to thine invoker give wealth in cattle, lasting, rich in marvels.
To us be born a son and spreading offspring. Agni, be this thy gracious will to us-ward.
