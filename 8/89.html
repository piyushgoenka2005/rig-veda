HYMN LXXXIX INDRA. VĀK.

बर्हदिन्द्रायbṛhadindrāya गायतghāyata मरुतोmaruto वर्त्रहन्तममvṛtrahantamam |

येनyena जयोतिरजनयन्न्र्ताव्र्धोjyotirajanayannṛtāvṛdho देवंdevaṃ देवायdevāya जाग्र्विjāghṛvi ||
I MOVE before thee here present in person, and all the Deities follow behind me.
When, Indra, thou securest me my portion, with me thou shalt perform heroic actions.
8.89.1
अपाधमदभिशस्तीरशस्तिहाथेन्द्रोapādhamadabhiśastīraśastihāthendro दयुम्न्याभवतdyumnyābhavat |

देवास्तdevāsta इन्द्रindra सख्यायsakhyāya येमिरेyemire बर्हद्भानोbṛhadbhāno मरुद्गणmarudghaṇa ||
The food of meath in foremost place I give thee, thy Soma shall be pressed, thy share appointed.
Thou on my right shalt be my friend and comrade: then shall we two smite dead full many a foeman.
8.89.2
परpra वva इन्द्रायindrāya बर्हतेbṛhate मरुतोmaruto बरह्मार्चतbrahmārcata |

वर्त्रंvṛtraṃ हनतिhanati वर्त्रहाvṛtrahā शतक्रतुर्वज्रेणśatakraturvajreṇa शतपर्वणाśataparvaṇā ||
Striving for strength bring forth a laud to Indra, a truthful hymn if he in truth existeth.
One and another say, There is no Indra. Who hath beheld him? Whom then shall we honour?
8.89.3
अभिabhi परpra भरbhara धर्षताdhṛṣatā धर्षन्मनःdhṛṣanmanaḥ शरवश्चितśravaścit तेte असदasad बर्हतbṛhat |

अर्षन्त्वापोarṣantvāpo जवसाjavasā विvi मातरोmātaro हनोhano वर्त्रंvṛtraṃ जयाjayā सवःsvaḥ ||
Here am I, look upon me here, O singer. All that existeth 1 surpass in greatness.
The Holy Law's commandments make me mighty. Rending with strength I rend the worlds asunder.
8.89.4
यज्जायथाyajjāyathā अपूर्व्यapūrvya मघवनmaghavan वर्त्रहत्यायvṛtrahatyāya |

ततtat पर्थिवीमप्रथयस्तदस्तभ्नाpṛthivīmaprathayastadastabhnā उतuta दयामdyām ||
When the Law's lovers mounted and ap. proached me as 1 sate lone upon the dear sky's summit.
Then spake my spirit to the heart within me, My friends have cried unto me with their children.
8.89.5
ततtat तेte यज्ञोyajño अजायतajāyata तदर्कtadarka उतuta हस्क्र्तिःhaskṛtiḥ |

तदtad विश्वमभिभूरसिviśvamabhibhūrasi यज्जातंyajjātaṃ यच्चyacca जन्त्वमjantvam ||
All these thy deeds must be declared at Soma-feasts, wrought, Indra, Bounteous Lord, for him who sheds the juice,
When thou didst open wealth heaped up by many, brought from far away to Sarablia, the Ṛṣi's kin.
8.89.6
आमासुāmāsu पक्वमैरयpakvamairaya आā सूर्यंsūryaṃ रोहयोrohayo दिविdivi |

घर्मंgharmaṃ नna सामनsāman तपताtapatā सुव्र्क्तिभिर्जुष्टंsuvṛktibhirjuṣṭaṃ गिर्वणसेghirvaṇase बर्हतbṛhat ||
Now run ye forth your several ways: he is not here who kept you back.
For hath not Indra sunk his bolt deep down in Vṛtra's vital part?
