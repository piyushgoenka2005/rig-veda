HYMN III. INDRA.

पिबाpibā सुतस्यsutasya रसिनोrasino मत्स्वाmatsvā नna इन्द्रindra गोमतःghomataḥ |

आपिर्नोāpirno बोधिसधमाद्योbodhisadhamādyo वर्धे.अस्मानवन्तुvṛdhe.asmānavantu तेte धियःdhiyaḥ ||
DRINK, Indra, of the savoury juice, and cheer thee with our milky draught.
Be, for our weal, our Friend and sharer of the feast, and let thy wisdom guard us well.
8.3.1
भूयामbhūyāma तेte सुमतौsumatau वाजिनोvājino वयंvayaṃ माmā नna सतरभिमातयेstarabhimātaye |

अस्माञ्चित्राभिरवतादभिष्टिभिराasmāñcitrābhiravatādabhiṣṭibhirā नःnaḥ सुम्नेषुsumneṣu यामयyāmaya ||
In thy kind grace and favour may we still be strong: expose us not to foe's attack.
With manifold assistance guard and succour us, and bring us to felicity.
8.3.2
इमाimā उu तवाtvā पुरूवसोpurūvaso गिरोghiro वर्धन्तुvardhantu याyā ममmama |

पावकवर्णाःशुचयोpāvakavarṇāḥśucayo विपश्चितो.अभिvipaścito.abhi सतोमैरनूषतstomairanūṣata ||
May these my songs of praise exalt thee, Lord, who hast abundant wealth.
Men skilled in holy hymns, pure, with the hues of fire, have sung them with their lauds to thee.
8.3.3
अयंayaṃ सहस्रंsahasraṃ रषिभिःṛṣibhiḥ सहस्क्र्तःsahaskṛtaḥ समुद्रsamudra इवiva पप्रथेpaprathe |

सत्यःsatyaḥ सोso अस्यasya महिमाmahimā गर्णेghṛṇe शवोśavo यज्ञेषुyajñeṣu विप्रराज्येviprarājye ||
He, with his might enhanced by Ṛṣis thousandfold, hath like an ocean spread himself.
His majesty is praised as true at solemn rites, his power where holy singers rule.
8.3.4
इन्द्रमिदindramid देवतातयdevatātaya इन्द्रंindraṃ परयत्यध्वरेprayatyadhvare |

इन्द्रंindraṃ समीकेवनिनोsamīkevanino हवामहhavāmaha इन्द्रंindraṃ धनस्यdhanasya सातयेsātaye ||
Indra for worship of the Gods, Indra while sacrifice proceeds,
Indra, as worshippers in battle-shock, we call, Indra that we may win the spoil.
8.3.5
इन्द्रोindro मह्नाmahnā रोदसीrodasī पप्रथच्छवpaprathacchava इन्द्रःindraḥ सूर्यमरोचयतsūryamarocayat |

इन्द्रेindre हha विश्वाviśvā भुवनानिbhuvanāni येमिरyemira इन्द्रेindre सुवानासsuvānāsa इन्दवःindavaḥ ||
With might hath Indra spread out heaven and earth, with power hath Indra lighted up the Sun.
In Indra are all creatures closely held; in him meet the distilling Soma-drops.
8.3.6
अभिabhi तवाtvā पूर्वपीतयpūrvapītaya इन्द्रindra सतोमेभिरायवःstomebhirāyavaḥ |

समीचीनासर्भवःsamīcīnāsaṛbhavaḥ समस्वरनsamasvaran रुद्राrudrā गर्नन्तghṛnanta पूर्व्यमpūrvyam ||
Men with their lauds are urging thee, Indra, to drink the Soma first.
The Ṛbhus in accord have lifted up their voice, and Rudras sung thee as the first.
8.3.7
अस्येदिन्द्रोasyedindro वाव्र्धेvāvṛdhe वर्ष्ण्यंvṛṣṇyaṃ शवोśavo मदेmade सुतस्यsutasya विष्णविviṣṇavi |

अद्याadyā तमस्यtamasya महिमानमायवो.अनुmahimānamāyavo.anu षटुवन्तिṣṭuvanti पूर्वथाpūrvathā ||
Indra increased his manly strength at sacrifice, in the wild rapture of this juice.
And living men to-day, even as of old, sing forth their praises to his majesty.
8.3.8
ततtat तवाtvā यामिyāmi सुवीर्यंsuvīryaṃ तदtad बरह्मbrahma पूर्वचित्तयेpūrvacittaye |

येनाyenā यतिभ्योyatibhyo भर्गवेbhṛghave धनेdhane हितेhite येनyena परस्कण्वमाविथpraskaṇvamāvitha ||
I crave of thee that hero strength, that thou mayst first regard this prayer,
Wherewith thou holpest Bhṛgu and the Yatis and Praskaṇva when the prize was staked.
8.3.9
येनाyenā समुद्रमस्र्जोsamudramasṛjo महीरपस्तदिन्द्रmahīrapastadindra वर्ष्णिvṛṣṇi तेte शवःśavaḥ |

सद्यःsadyaḥ सोso अस्यasya महिमाmahimā नna संनशेsaṃnaśe यंyaṃ कषोणीरनुचक्रदेkṣoṇīranucakrade ||
Wherewith thou sentest mighty waters to the sea, that, Indra, is thy manly strength.
For ever unattainable is this power of him to whom the worlds have cried aloud.
8.3.10
शग्धीśaghdhī नna इन्द्रindra यतyat तवाtvā रयिंrayiṃ यामिyāmi सुवीर्यमsuvīryam |

शग्धिśaghdhi वाजायvājāya परथमंprathamaṃ सिषासतेsiṣāsate शग्धिśaghdhi सतोमायstomāya पूर्व्यpūrvya ||
Help us, O Indra, when we pray to thee for wealth and hero might.
First help thou on to strength the man who strives to win, and aid our laud, O Ancient One.
8.3.11
शग्धीśaghdhī नोno अस्यasya यदyad धdha पौरमाविथpauramāvitha धियdhiya इन्द्रindra सिषासतःsiṣāsataḥ |

शग्धिśaghdhi यथाyathā रुशमंruśamaṃ शयावकंśyāvakaṃ कर्पमिन्द्रkṛpamindra परावःprāvaḥ सवर्णरमsvarṇaram ||
Help for us, Indra, as thou holpest Paura once, this man's devotions bent on gain.
Help, as thou gavest Ruśama and Śyāvaka and Svarṇara and Kṛpa aid.
8.3.12
कनkan नव्योnavyo अतसीनांatasīnāṃ तुरोturo गर्णीतghṛṇīta मर्त्यःmartyaḥ |

नहीnahī नवस्यnvasya महिमानमिन्द्रियंmahimānamindriyaṃ सवर्ग्र्णन्तsvarghṛṇanta आनशुःānaśuḥ ||
What newest of imploring prayers shall, then, the zealous mortal sing?
For have not they who laud his might, and Indra-power won for themselves the light of heaven?
8.3.13
कदुkadu सतुवन्तstuvanta रतयन्तṛtayanta देवतdevata रषिःṛṣiḥ कोko विप्रvipra ओहतेohate |

कदाkadā हवंhavaṃ मघवन्निन्द्रmaghavannindra सुन्वतःsunvataḥ कदुkadu सतुवतstuvata आā गमःghamaḥ ||
When shall they keep the Law and praise thee mid the Gods? Who counts as Ṛṣi and as sage?
When ever wilt thou, Indra Maghavan, come nigh to presser's or to praiser's call?
8.3.14
उदुudu तयेtye मधुमत्तमाmadhumattamā गिरghira सतोमासstomāsa ईरतेīrate |

सत्राजितोsatrājito धनसाdhanasā अक्षितोतयोakṣitotayo वाजयन्तोvājayanto रथाrathā इवiva ||
These songs of ours exceeding sweet, these hymns of praise ascend to thee,
Like ever-conquering chariots that display their strength, gain wealth, and give unfailing aid.
8.3.15
कण्वाkaṇvā इवiva भर्गवःbhṛghavaḥ सूर्याsūryā इवiva विश्वमिदviśvamid धीतमानशुःdhītamānaśuḥ |

इन्द्रंindraṃ सतोमेभिर्महयन्तstomebhirmahayanta आयवःāyavaḥ परियमेधासोpriyamedhāso अस्वरनasvaran ||
The Bhṛgus are like Suns, like Kaṇvas, and have gained all that their thoughts were bent upon.
The living men of Priyamedha's race have sung exalting Indra with their lauds.
8.3.16
युक्ष्वाyukṣvā हिhi वर्त्रहन्तमvṛtrahantama हरीharī इन्द्रindra परावतःparāvataḥ |

अर्वाचीनोarvācīno मघवनmaghavan सोमपीतयsomapītaya उग्रughra रष्वेभिराṛṣvebhirā गहिghahi ||
Best slayer of the Vṛtras, yoke thy Bay Steeds, Indra, from afar.
Come with the High Ones hither, Maghavan, to us, Mighty, to drink the Soma juice.
8.3.17
इमेime हिhi तेte कारवोkāravo वावशुर्धियाvāvaśurdhiyā विप्रासोviprāso मेधसातयेmedhasātaye |

सत्वंsatvaṃ नोno मघवन्निन्द्रmaghavannindra गिर्वणोghirvaṇo वेनोveno नna शर्णुधीśṛṇudhī हवमhavam ||
For these, the bards and singers, have cried out to thee with prayer, to gain the sacrifice.
As such, O Maghavan, Indra, who lovest song, even as a lover bear my call.
8.3.18
निरिन्द्रnirindra बर्हतीभ्योbṛhatībhyo वर्त्रंvṛtraṃ धनुभ्योdhanubhyo अस्फुरःasphuraḥ |

निरर्बुदस्यnirarbudasya मर्गयस्यmṛghayasya मायिनोmāyino निःniḥ पर्वतस्यparvatasya गाghā आजःājaḥ ||
Thou from the lofty plains above, O Indra, hurledst Vṛtra down.
Thou dravest forth the kine of guileful Mṛgaya and Arbuda from the mountain's hold.
8.3.19
निरग्नयोniraghnayo रुरुचुर्निरुrurucurniru सूर्योsūryo निःniḥ सोमsoma इन्द्रियोindriyo रसःrasaḥ |

निरन्तरिक्षादधमोnirantarikṣādadhamo महामहिंmahāmahiṃ कर्षेkṛṣe तदिन्द्रtadindra पौंस्यमpauṃsyam ||
Bright were the flaming fires, the Sun gave forth his shine, and Soma, Indra's juice, shone clear.
Indra, thou blewest the great Dragon from the air: men must regard that valorous deed.
8.3.20
यंyaṃ मेme दुरिन्द्रोdurindro मरुतःmarutaḥ पाकस्थामाpākasthāmā कौरयाणःkaurayāṇaḥ |

विश्वेषांviśveṣāṃ तमनाtmanā शोभिष्ठमुपेवśobhiṣṭhamupeva दिविdivi धावमानमdhāvamānam ||
The fairest courser of them all, who runneth on as ’twere to heaven.
Which Indra and the Maruts gave, and Pākasthāman Kaurayāṇ.
8.3.21
रोहितंrohitaṃ मेme पाकस्थामाpākasthāmā सुधुरंsudhuraṃ कक्ष्यप्रामkakṣyaprām |

अदादadād रायोrāyo विबोधनमvibodhanam ||
To me hath Pākasthāman given, a ruddy horse, good at the pole,
Filling is girth and rousing wealth;
8.3.22
यस्माyasmā अन्येanye दशdaśa परतिprati धुरंdhuraṃ वहन्तिvahanti वह्नयःvahnayaḥ |

अस्तंastaṃ वयोvayo नna तुग्र्यमtughryam ||
Compared with whom no other ten strong coursers, harnessed to the pole,
Bear Tugrya to his dwelling place.
8.3.23
आत्माātmā पितुस्तनूर्वासpitustanūrvāsa ओजोदाojodā अभ्यञ्जनमabhyañjanam |

तुरीयमिदturīyamid रोहितस्यrohitasya पाकस्थामानंpākasthāmānaṃ भोजंbhojaṃ दातारमब्रवमdātāramabravam ||
Raiment is body, food is life, and healing ointment giveth strength.
As the free-handed giver of the ruddy steed, I have named Pākasthāman fourth.
8.3.24
