HYMN CLXXXVI. VĀYU.

वातvāta आā वातुvātu भेषजंbheṣajaṃ शम्भुśambhu मयोभुmayobhu नोno हर्देhṛde |

परpra णायूंषिṇaāyūṃṣi तारिषतtāriṣat ||
FILLING our hearts with health and joy, may Vāta breathe his balm on us
May he prolong our days of life.
10.186.1
उतuta वातvāta पितासिpitāsi नna उतuta भरातोतbhrātota नःnaḥ सखाsakhā |

सsa नोजीवातवेnojīvātave कर्धिkṛdhi ||
Thou art our Father, Vāta, yea, thou art a Brother and a friend,
So give us strength that we may live.
10.186.2
यददोyadado वातvāta तेte गर्हे.अम्र्तस्यghṛhe.amṛtasya निधिर्हितःnidhirhitaḥ |

ततोtato नोno देहिजीवसेdehijīvase ||
The store of Amṛta laid away yonder, O Vāta, in thine home,—
Give us thereof that we may live.
10.186.3
