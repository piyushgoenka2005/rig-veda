HYMN CXIX. INDRA.

इतिiti वाvā इतिiti मेme मनोmano गामश्वंghāmaśvaṃ सनुयामितिsanuyāmiti |

कुवित्सोमस्यापामितिkuvitsomasyāpāmiti ||
THIS, even this was my resolve, to win a cow, to win a steed:
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.1
परpra वाताvātā इवiva दोधतdodhata उनun माmā पीताpītā अयंसतayaṃsata |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
Like violent gusts of wind the draughts that I have drunk have lifted me
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.2
उनun माmā पीताpītā अयंसतayaṃsata रथमश्वाrathamaśvā इवाशवःivāśavaḥ |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
The draughts I drank have borne me up, as fleet-foot horses draw a car:
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.3
उपupa माmā मतिरस्थितmatirasthita वाश्राvāśrā पुत्रमिवputramiva परियमpriyam |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
The hymn hath reached me, like a cow who lows to meet her darling calf:
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.4
अहंahaṃ तष्टेवtaṣṭeva वन्धुरंvandhuraṃ पर्यचामिparyacāmi हर्दाhṛdā मतिमmatim |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
As a wright bends a chariot-seat so round my heart I bend the hymn:
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.5
नहिnahi मेme अक्षिपच्चनाछान्त्सुःakṣipaccanāchāntsuḥ पञ्चpañca कर्ष्टयःkṛṣṭayaḥ |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
Not as a mote within the eye count the Five Tribes of men with me:
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.6
नहिnahi मेme रोदसीrodasī उभेubhe अन्यंanyaṃ पक्षंpakṣaṃ चनcana परतिprati |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
The heavens and earth themselves have not grown equal to one half of me
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.7
अभिabhi दयांdyāṃ महिनाmahinā भुवमभीमांbhuvamabhīmāṃ पर्थिवींpṛthivīṃ महीमmahīm |

कुवित...kuvit... ||
I in my grandeur have surpassed the heavens and all this spacious earth
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.8
हन्ताहंhantāhaṃ पर्थिवीमिमांpṛthivīmimāṃ निni दधानीहdadhānīha वेहveha वाvā |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
Aha! this spacious earth will I deposit either here or there
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.9
ओषमितoṣamit पर्थिवीमहंpṛthivīmahaṃ जङघनानीहjaṅghanānīha वेहveha वाvā |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
In one short moment will I smite the earth in fury here or there:
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.10
दिविdivi मेme अन्यःanyaḥ पक्षो.अधोpakṣo.adho अन्यमचीक्र्षमanyamacīkṛṣam |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
One of my flanks is in the sky; I let the other trail below:
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.11
अहमस्मिahamasmi महामहो.अभिनभ्यमुदीषितःmahāmaho.abhinabhyamudīṣitaḥ |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
1, greatest of the Mighty Ones, am lifted to the firmament:
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
10.119.12
गर्होghṛho याम्यरंक्र्तोyāmyaraṃkṛto देवेभ्योdevebhyo हव्यवाहनःhavyavāhanaḥ |

कुवितkuvit ...... ||
I seek the worshipper's abode; oblation-bearer to the Gods:
Have I not drunk of Soma juice?
