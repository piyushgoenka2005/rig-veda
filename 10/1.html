HYMN I. AGNI.

अग्रेaghre बर्हन्नुषसामूर्ध्वोbṛhannuṣasāmūrdhvo अस्थानasthān निर्जगन्वानnirjaghanvān तमसोज्योतिषागातtamasojyotiṣāghāt |

अग्निर्भानुनाaghnirbhānunā रुशताruśatā सवङगsvaṅgha आā जातोविश्वाjātoviśvā सद्मान्यप्राःsadmānyaprāḥ ||
HIGH hath the Mighty risen before the dawning, and come to us with light from out the darkness.
Fair-shapen Agni with white-shining splendour hath filled at birth all human habitations.
10.1.1
सsa जातोjāto गर्भोgharbho असिasi रोदस्योरग्नेrodasyoraghne चारुर्विभ्र्तcārurvibhṛta ओषधीषुoṣadhīṣu |

चित्रःcitraḥ शिशुःśiśuḥ परिpari तमांस्यक्तूनtamāṃsyaktūn परpra मात्र्भ्योmātṛbhyo अधिकनिक्रदतadhikanikradat गाःghāḥ ||
Thou, being born, art Child of Earth and Heaven, parted among the plants in beauty, Agni!
The glooms of night thou, Brilliant Babe, subduest, and art come forth, loud roaring, from thy Mothers.
10.1.2
विष्णुरित्थाviṣṇuritthā परममस्यparamamasya विद्वाञvidvāñ जातोjāto बर्हन्नभिbṛhannabhi पातित्र्तीयमpātitṛtīyam |

आसाāsā यदस्यyadasya पयोpayo अक्रतakrata सवंsvaṃ सचेतसोsacetaso अभ्यर्चन्त्यत्रabhyarcantyatra ||
Here, being manifested, lofty Viṣṇu, full wise, protects his own supremest station.
When they have offered in his mouth their sweet milk, to him with one accord they sing forth praises.
10.1.3
अतata उu तवाtvā पितुभ्र्तोpitubhṛto जनित्रीरन्नाव्र्धंjanitrīrannāvṛdhaṃ परतिprati चरन्त्यन्नैःcarantyannaiḥ |

ताtā ईंīṃ परत्येषिpratyeṣi पुनरन्यरूपाpunaranyarūpā असिasi तवंtvaṃ विक्षुमानुषीषुvikṣumānuṣīṣu होताhotā ||
Thence bearing food the Mothers come to meet thee, with food for thee who givest food its increase.
These in their altered form again thou meetest. Thou art Invoking Priest in homes of mortals.
10.1.4
होतारंhotāraṃ चित्ररथमध्वरस्यcitrarathamadhvarasya यज्ञस्य-यज्ञस्यyajñasya-yajñasya केतुंरुशन्तमketuṃruśantam |

परत्यर्धिंpratyardhiṃ देवस्य-देवस्यdevasya-devasya मह्नाmahnā शरियाśriyā तवग्निमतिथिंtvaghnimatithiṃ जनानामjanānām ||
Priest of the holy rite, with car that glitters, refulgent Banner of each act of worship,
Sharinging every God through might and glory, even Agni Guest of men I summon hither.
10.1.5
सsa तुtu वस्त्राण्यधvastrāṇyadha पेशनानिpeśanāni वसानोvasāno अग्निर्नाभाप्र्थिव्याःaghnirnābhāpṛthivyāḥ |

अरुषोaruṣo जातःjātaḥ पदpada इळायाःiḷāyāḥ पुरोहितोpurohito राजन्यक्षीहrājanyakṣīha देवानdevān ||
So Agni stands on earth's most central station, invested in well-decorated garments.
Born, red of hue, where men pour out libations, O King, as great High Priest bring the Gods hither.
10.1.6
आā हिhi दयावाप्र्थिवीdyāvāpṛthivī अग्नaghna उभेubhe सदाsadā पुत्रोputro नna मातराततन्थmātarātatantha |

परpra याह्यछोशतोyāhyachośato यविष्ठाथाyaviṣṭhāthā वहvaha सहस्येहदेवानsahasyehadevān ||
Over the earth and over heaven, O Agni, thou, Son, hast ever spread above thy Parents.
Come, Youthfullest! to those who long to meet thee, and hither bring the Gods, O Mighty Victor.
10.1.7
