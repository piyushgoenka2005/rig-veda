HYMN XLIV. INDRA.

आā यात्विन्द्रःyātvindraḥ सवपतिर्मदायsvapatirmadāya योyo धर्मणाdharmaṇā तूतुजानस्तुविष्मानtūtujānastuviṣmān |

परत्वक्षाणोpratvakṣāṇo अतिati विश्वाviśvā सहांस्यपारेणमहताsahāṃsyapāreṇamahatā वर्ष्ण्येनvṛṣṇyena ||
MAY Sovran Indra come to the carousal, he who by Holy Law is strong and active,
The overcomer of all conquering forces with his great steer-like power that hath no limit.
10.44.1
सुष्ठामाsuṣṭhāmā रथःrathaḥ सुयमाsuyamā हरीharī तेte मिम्यक्षmimyakṣa वज्रोvajro नर्पतेगभस्तौnṛpateghabhastau |

शीभंśībhaṃ राजनrājan सुपथाsupathā याह्यर्वांyāhyarvāṃ वर्धामतेvardhāmate पपुषोpapuṣo वर्ष्ण्यानिvṛṣṇyāni ||
Firm-seated is thy car, thy Steeds are docile; thy hand, O King, holds, firmly grasped, the thunder.
On thy fair path, O Lord of men, come quickly: we will increase thy powers when thou hast drunken.
10.44.2
एन्द्रवाहोendravāho नर्पतिंnṛpatiṃ वज्रबाहुमुग्रमुग्रासस्तविषासvajrabāhumughramughrāsastaviṣāsa एनमenam |

परत्वक्षसंpratvakṣasaṃ वर्षभंvṛṣabhaṃ सत्यशुष्ममेमस्मत्राsatyaśuṣmamemasmatrā सधमादोवहन्तुsadhamādovahantu ||
Let strong and mighty Steeds who bear this Mighty Indra, the Lord of men, whose arm wields thunder,
Bring unto us, as sharers of our banquet, the Steer of conquering might, of real vigour.
10.44.3
एवाevā पतिंpatiṃ दरोणसाचंdroṇasācaṃ सचेतसमूर्जsacetasamūrja सकम्भंskambhaṃ धरुणाdharuṇaā वर्षायसेvṛṣāyase |

ओजःojaḥ कर्ष्वkṛṣva संsaṃ गर्भायghṛbhāya तवेtve अप्यसोapyaso यथाकेनिपानामिनोyathākenipānāmino वर्धेvṛdhe ||
So like a Bull thou rushest to the Lord who loves the trough, the Sage, the prop of vigour, in the vat,
Prepare thine energies, collect them in thyself: be for our profit as the Master of the wise.
10.44.4
गमन्नस्मेghamannasme वसून्याvasūnyā हिhi शंसिषंśaṃsiṣaṃ सवाशिषंsvāśiṣaṃ भरमायाहिbharamāyāhi सोमिनःsominaḥ |

तवमीशिषेtvamīśiṣe सास्मिन्नाsāsminnā सत्सिsatsi बर्हिष्यनाध्र्ष्याbarhiṣyanādhṛṣyā तवtava पात्राणिpātrāṇi धर्मणाdharmaṇā ||
May precious treasures come to us-so will I pray. Come to the votary's gift offered with beauteous laud.
Thou art the Lord, as such sit on this holy grass: thy vessels are inviolate as Law commands.
10.44.5
पर्थकpṛthak परायनprāyan परथमाprathamā देवहूतयो.अक्र्ण्वतdevahūtayo.akṛṇvata शरवस्यानिदुष्टराśravasyāniduṣṭarā |

नna येye शेकुर्यज्ञियांśekuryajñiyāṃ नावमारुहमीर्मैवतेnāvamāruhamīrmaivate नयविशन्तnyaviśanta केपयःkepayaḥ ||
Far went our earlist invocation of the Gods, and won us glories that can never be surpassed.
They who could not ascend the ship of sacrifice, sink down in desolation, trembling with alarm.
10.44.6
एवैवापागपरेevaivāpāghapare सन्तुsantu दूढ्यो.अश्वाdūḍhyo.aśvā येषांyeṣāṃ दुर्युजायुयुज्रेduryujaāyuyujre |

इत्थाitthā येye परागुपरेprāghupare सन्तिsanti दावनेdāvane पुरूणियत्रpurūṇiyatra वयुनानिvayunāni भोजनाbhojanā ||
So be the others, evil-hearted, far away, whose horses, difficult to harness, have been yoked.
Here in advance men stand anear to offer gifts, by whom full many a work that brings reward is done.
10.44.7
गिरीन्रज्रानghirīnrajrān रेजमानानधारयदrejamānānadhārayad दयौःdyauḥ करन्ददन्तरिक्षाणिkrandadantarikṣāṇi कोपयतkopayat |

समीचीनेsamīcīne धिषणेdhiṣaṇe विvi षकभायतिव्र्ष्णःṣkabhāyativṛṣṇaḥ पीत्वाpītvā मदmada उक्थानिukthāni शंसतिśaṃsati ||
He firmly fixed the plains and mountains as they shook. Dyaus thundered forth and made the air's mid-region quake.
He stays apart the two confronting bowls; he sings lauds in the potent Soma's joy when he hath drunk.
10.44.8
इमंimaṃ बिभर्मिbibharmi सुक्र्तंsukṛtaṃ तेte अङकुशंaṅkuśaṃ येनारुजासिyenārujāsi मघवञ्छफारुजःmaghavañchaphārujaḥ |

अस्मिनasmin सुsu तेte सवनेsavane अस्त्वोक्यंastvokyaṃ सुतsuta इष्टौमघवनiṣṭaumaghavan बोध्याभगःbodhyābhaghaḥ ||
I bear this deftly-fashioned goad of thine, wherewith thou, Maghavan, shalt break the strikers with the hoof.
At this libation mayst thou be well satisfied. Partake the juice, partake the worship, Maghavan.
10.44.9
गोभिषghobhiṣ टरेमामतिंṭaremāmatiṃ ...... ||
O Much-invoked, may we subdue all famine and evil want with store of grain and cattle.
May we allied, as first in rank, with princes obtain possessions by our own exertion.
10.44.10
बर्हस्पतिर्नःbṛhaspatirnaḥ परिpari ...... ||
Bṛhaspati protect us from the rearward, and from above, and from below, from sinners.
May Indra from the front and from the centre, as Friend to friends, vouchsafe us room and freedom.
10.44.11
