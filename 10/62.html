HYMN LXII. VIŚVEDEVAS, OTHERS.

येye यज्ञेनyajñena दक्षिणयाdakṣiṇayā समक्ताsamaktā इन्द्रस्यindrasya सख्यमम्र्तत्वमानशsakhyamamṛtatvamānaśa |

तेभ्योtebhyo भद्रमङगिरसोbhadramaṅghiraso वोvo अस्तुastu परतिprati गर्भ्णीतमानवंghṛbhṇītamānavaṃ सुमेधसःsumedhasaḥ ||
YE, who, adorned with guerdon through the sacrifice, have won you Indra's friendship and eternal life,
Even to you be happiness, Aṅgirases. Welcome the son of Manu, ye who are most wise.
10.62.1
यya उदाजनudājan पितरोpitaro गोमयंghomayaṃ वस्वvasv रतेनाभिन्दनṛtenābhindan परिवत्सरेवलमparivatsarevalam |

दीर्घायुत्वमङगिरसोdīrghāyutvamaṅghiraso वोvo अस्तुastu परतिprati गर्भ्णीतमानवंghṛbhṇītamānavaṃ सुमेधसःsumedhasaḥ ||
The Fathers, who drave forth the wealth in cattle, have in the year's courses cleft Vala by Eternal Law:
A lengthened life be yours, O ye Aṅgirases. Welcome the son of Manu, ye who are most wise.
10.62.2
यya रतेनṛtena सूर्यमारोहयनsūryamārohayan दिव्यप्रथयनdivyaprathayan पर्थिवींpṛthivīṃ मातरंविmātaraṃvi |

सुप्रजास्त्वमङगिरसोsuprajāstvamaṅghiraso वोvo अस्तुastu परतिprati गर्भ्णीतghṛbhṇīta मानवंसुमेधसःmānavaṃsumedhasaḥ ||
Ye raised the Sun to heaven by everlasting Law, and spread broad earth, the Mother, out on every side.
Fair wealth of progeny be yours, Aṅgirases. Welcome the son of Manu, ye who are most wise.
10.62.3
अयंayaṃ नाभाnābhā वदतिvadati वल्गुvalghu वोvo गर्हेghṛhe देवपुत्राdevaputrā रषयस्तच्छ्र्णोतनṛṣayastacchṛṇotana |

सुब्रह्मण्यमङगिरसोsubrahmaṇyamaṅghiraso वोvo अस्तुastu परतिprati गर्भ्णीतमानवंghṛbhṇītamānavaṃ सुमेधसःsumedhasaḥ ||
This kinsman in your dwellingplace speaks pleasant words: give car to this, ye Ṛṣis, children of the Gods.
High Brahman dignity be yours, Aṅgirases. Welcome the son of Manu, ye who are most wise.
10.62.4
विरूपासvirūpāsa इदid रषयस्तṛṣayasta इदid गम्भीरवेपसःghambhīravepasaḥ |

तेte अङगिरसःसूनवस्तेaṅghirasaḥsūnavaste अग्नेःaghneḥ परिpari जज्ञिरेjajñire ||
Distinguished by their varied form, these Ṛṣis have been deeply moved.
These are the sons of Aṅgirases: from Agni have they sprung to life.
10.62.5
येye अग्नेःaghneḥ परिpari जज्ञिरेjajñire विरूपासोvirūpāso दिवसdivas परिpari |

नवग्वोnavaghvo नुदशग्वोnudaśaghvo अङगिरस्तमोaṅghirastamo सचाsacā देवेषुdeveṣu मंअतेmaṃate ||
Distinguished by their varied form, they sprang from Agni, from the sky.
Navagva and Daśagva, noblest Aṅgiras, he giveth bounty with the Gods.
10.62.6
इन्द्रेणindreṇa युजाyujā निःniḥ सर्जन्तsṛjanta वाघतोvāghato वरजंvrajaṃ गोमन्तमश्विनमghomantamaśvinam |

सहस्रंsahasraṃ मेme ददतोdadato अष्टकर्ण्यःaṣṭakarṇyaḥ शरवोśravo देवेष्वक्रतdeveṣvakrata ||
With Indra for associate the priests have cleared the stable full of steeds and kine,
Giving to me a thousand with their eightmarked cars, they gained renown among the Gods.
10.62.7
परpra नूनंnūnaṃ जायतामयंjāyatāmayaṃ मनुस्तोक्मेवmanustokmeva रोहतुrohatu |

यःyaḥ सहस्रंशताश्वंsahasraṃśatāśvaṃ सद्योsadyo दानायdānāya मंहतेmaṃhate ||
May this man's sons be multiplied; like springing corn may Manu grow,
Who gives at once in bounteous gift a thousand kine, a hundred steeds.
10.62.8
नna तमश्नोतिtamaśnoti कश्चनkaścana दिवdiva इवiva सान्वारभमsānvārabham |

सावर्ण्यस्यsāvarṇyasya दक्षिणाdakṣiṇā विvi सिन्धुरिवsindhuriva पप्रथेpaprathe ||
No one attains to him, as though a man would grasp the heights of heaven.
Savarnya's sacrificial meed hath broadened like an ample flood.
10.62.9
उतuta दासाdāsā परिविषेpariviṣe समद्दिष्टीsmaddiṣṭī गोपरीणसाghoparīṇasā |

यदुस्तुर्वश्चyadusturvaśca मामहेmāmahe ||
Yadu and Turva, too, have given two Dāsas, well-disposed, to serve,
Together with great store of kine.
10.62.10
सहस्रदाsahasradā गरामणीर्माghrāmaṇīrmā रिषनriṣan मनुःmanuḥ सूर्येणास्ययतमानैतुsūryeṇāsyayatamānaitu दक्षिणाdakṣiṇā |

सावर्णेर्देवाःsāvarṇerdevāḥ परpra तिरन्त्वायुर्यस्मिन्नश्रान्ताtirantvāyuryasminnaśrāntā असनामasanāma वाजमvājam ||
Blest be the hamlet's chief, most liberal Manu, and may his bounty rival that of Sūrya.
May the God let Ssvarni's life be leng
thened, with whom, unwearied, we have lived and prospered.
10.62.11
