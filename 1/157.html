HYMN CLVII. AŚVINS.

अबोध्यग्निर्ज्मabodhyaghnirjma उदेतिudeti सूर्योsūryo वयुषाश्चन्द्राvyuṣāścandrā मह्यावोmahyāvo अर्चिषाarciṣā |

आयुक्षातामश्विनाāyukṣātāmaśvinā यातवेyātave रथंrathaṃ परासावीदprāsāvīd देवःdevaḥ सविताsavitā जगतjaghat पर्थकpṛthak ||
AGNI is wakened: Sūrya riseth from the earth. Mighty, refulgent Dawn hath shone with all her light.
The Aśvins have equipped their chariot for the course. God Savitar hath moved the folk in sundry ways.
1.157.1
यदyad युञ्जाथेyuñjāthe वर्षणमश्विनाvṛṣaṇamaśvinā रथंrathaṃ घर्तेनghṛtena नोno मधुनाmadhunā कषत्रमुक्षतमkṣatramukṣatam |

अस्माकंasmākaṃ बरह्मbrahma पर्तनासुpṛtanāsu जिन्वतंjinvataṃ वयंvayaṃ धनाdhanā शूरसाताśūrasātā भजेमहिbhajemahi ||
When, Aśvins, ye equip your very mighty car, bedew, ye Twain, our power with honey and with oil.
To our devotion give victorious strength in war: may we win riches in the heroes' strife for spoil.
1.157.2
अर्वांarvāṃ तरिचक्रोtricakro मधुवाहनोmadhuvāhano रथोratho जीराश्वोjīrāśvo अश्विनोर्यातुaśvinoryātu सुष्टुतःsuṣṭutaḥ |

तरिवन्धुरोtrivandhuro मघवाmaghavā विश्वसौभगःviśvasaubhaghaḥ शंśaṃ नna आā वक्षदvakṣad दविपदेdvipade चतुष्पदेcatuṣpade ||
Nigh to us come the Aśvins' lauded three-wheeled car, the car laden with meath and drawn by fleet-foot steeds,
Three-seated, opulent, bestowing all delight. may it bring weal to us, to cattle and to men.
1.157.3
आā नna ऊर्जंūrjaṃ वहतमश्विनाvahatamaśvinā युवंyuvaṃ मधुमत्याmadhumatyā नःnaḥ कशयाkaśayā मिमिक्षतमmimikṣatam |

परायुस्तारिष्टंprāyustāriṣṭaṃ नीnī रपांसिrapāṃsi मर्क्षतंmṛkṣataṃ सेधतंsedhataṃ दवेषोdveṣo भवतंbhavataṃ सचाभुवाsacābhuvā ||
Bring hither nourishment for us, ye Aśvins Twain; sprinkle us with your whip that drops with honey-dew.
Prolong our days of life, wipe out our trespasses; destroy our foes, be our companions and our Friends.
1.157.4
युवंyuvaṃ हha गर्भंgharbhaṃ जगतीषुjaghatīṣu धत्थोdhattho युवंyuvaṃ विश्वेषुviśveṣu भुवनेष्वन्तःbhuvaneṣvantaḥ |

युवमग्निंyuvamaghniṃ चca वर्षणावपश्चvṛṣaṇāvapaśca वनस्पतीन्रश्विनावैरयेथामvanaspatīnraśvināvairayethām ||
Ye store the germ of life in female creatures, ye lay it up within all living beings.
Ye have sent forth, O Aśvins passing mighty, the fire, the sovrans of the wood, the waters,
1.157.5
युवंyuvaṃ हha सथोstho भिषजाbhiṣajā भेषजेभिरथोbheṣajebhiratho हha सथोstho रथ्याrathyā राथ्येभिःrāthyebhiḥ |

अथोatho हha कषत्रमधिkṣatramadhi धत्थdhattha उग्राughrā योyo वांvāṃ हविष्मान्मनसाhaviṣmānmanasā ददाशdadāśa ||
Leeches are ye with medicines to heal us, and charioteers are ye with skill in driving.
Ye Strong, give sway to him who brings oblation and with his heart pours out his gift before you.
1.157.6
