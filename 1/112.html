HYMN CXII. AŚVINS.

ईळेīḷe दयावाप्र्थिवीdyāvāpṛthivī पूर्वचित्तये.अग्निंpūrvacittaye.aghniṃ घर्मंgharmaṃ सुरुचंsurucaṃ यामन्निष्टयेyāmanniṣṭaye |

याभिर्भरेyābhirbhare कारमंशायkāramaṃśāya जिन्वथस्ताभिरूjinvathastābhirū षुṣu ऊतिभिरश्विनाūtibhiraśvinā गतमghatam ||
To give first thought to them, I worship Heaven and Earth, and Agni, fair bright glow, to hasten their approach.
Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids wherewith in fight ye speed the war-cry to the spoil.
1.112.1
युवोर्दानायyuvordānāya सुभराsubharā असश्चतोasaścato रथमाrathamā तस्थुर्वचसंtasthurvacasaṃ नna मन्तवेmantave |

याभिर्धियो.अवथःकर्मन्निष्टयेyābhirdhiyo.avathaḥkarmanniṣṭaye ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Ample, unfailing, they have mounted as it were an eloquent car that ye may think of us and give.
Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids wherewith ye help our thoughts to further holy acts.
1.112.2
युवंyuvaṃ तासांtāsāṃ दिव्यस्यdivyasya परशासनेpraśāsane विशांviśāṃ कषयथोkṣayatho अम्र्तस्यमज्मनाamṛtasyamajmanā |

याभिर्धेनुमस्वंyābhirdhenumasvaṃ पिन्वथोpinvatho नराnarā ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Ye by the might which heavenly nectar giveth you are in supreme dominion Lords of all these folk.
Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids wherewith ye, Heroes, made the barren cow give milk.
1.112.3
याभिःyābhiḥ परिज्माparijmā तनयस्यtanayasya मज्मनाmajmanā दविमाताdvimātā तूर्षुtūrṣu तरणिर्विभूषतिtaraṇirvibhūṣati |

याभिस्त्रिमन्तुरभवदyābhistrimanturabhavad विचक्षणस्ताभिर..vicakṣaṇastābhir.. .. ||
The aids wherewith the Wanderer through his offspring's might, or the Two-Mothered Son shows swiftest mid the swift;
Wherewith the sapient one acquired his triple lore,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.4
याभीyābhī रेभंrebhaṃ निव्र्तंnivṛtaṃ सितमद्भ्यsitamadbhya उदud वन्दनमैरयतंvandanamairayataṃ सवर्द्र्शेsvardṛśe |

याभिःyābhiḥ कण्वंkaṇvaṃ परpra सिषासन्तमावतंsiṣāsantamāvataṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye raised from waters, prisoned and fast bound, Rebha, and Vandana to look upon the light;
Wherewith ye succoured Kaṇva as he strove to win,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.5
याभिरन्तकंyābhirantakaṃ जसमानमारणेjasamānamāraṇe भुज्यंbhujyaṃ याभिरव्यथिभिर्जिजिन्वथुःyābhiravyathibhirjijinvathuḥ |

याभिःyābhiḥ कर्कन्धुंkarkandhuṃ वय्यंvayyaṃ चca जिन्वथस्ताभिर...jinvathastābhir... ||
Wherewith ye rescued Antaka when languishing deep in the pit, and Bhujyu with unfailing help.
And comforted Karkandhu, Vayya, in their woe,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.6
याभिःyābhiḥ शुचन्तिंśucantiṃ धनसांdhanasāṃ सुषंसदंsuṣaṃsadaṃ तप्तंtaptaṃ घर्ममोम्यावन्तमत्रयेgharmamomyāvantamatraye |

याभिःyābhiḥ पर्ष्निगुंpṛṣnighuṃ पुरुकुत्समावतंpurukutsamāvataṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye gave Śucanti wealth and happy home, and made the fiery pit friendly for Atri's sake;
Wherewith ye guarded Purukutsa, Pṛśnigu,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvin;, with those aids.
1.112.7
याभिःyābhiḥ शचीभिर्व्र्षणाśacībhirvṛṣaṇā पराव्र्जंparāvṛjaṃ परान्धंprāndhaṃ शरोणंśroṇaṃ चक्षसcakṣasa एतवेetave कर्थःkṛthaḥ |

याभिर्वर्तिकांyābhirvartikāṃ गरसिताममुञ्चतन्ताभिर...ghrasitāmamuñcatantābhir... ||
Mighty Ones, with what powers ye gave Parāvṛj aid what time ye made the blind and lame to see and walk;
Wherewith ye set at liberty the swallowed quail,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.8
याभिःyābhiḥ सिन्धुंsindhuṃ मधुमन्तमसश्चतंmadhumantamasaścataṃ वसिष्ठंvasiṣṭhaṃ याभिरजरावजिन्वतमyābhirajarāvajinvatam |

याभिःyābhiḥ कुत्संkutsaṃ शरुतर्यंśrutaryaṃ नर्यमावतंnaryamāvataṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye quickened the most sweet exhaustless flood, and comforted Vasiṣṭha, ye who ne’er decay;
And to Śrutarya, Kutsa, Narya gave your help,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.9
याभिर्विश्पलांyābhirviśpalāṃ धनसामथर्व्यंdhanasāmatharvyaṃ सहस्रमीळ्हsahasramīḷha आजावजिन्वतमājāvajinvatam |

याभिर्वशमश्व्यंyābhirvaśamaśvyaṃ परेणिमावतंpreṇimāvataṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye helped, in battle of a thousand spoils, Viśpalā seeking booty, powerless to move.
Wherewith ye guarded friendly Vaśa, Aśva's son,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.10
याभिःyābhiḥ सुदानूsudānū औशिजायauśijāya वणिजेvaṇije दीर्घश्रवसेdīrghaśravase मधुmadhu कोशोक्षरतkośoakṣarat |

कक्षीवन्तंkakṣīvantaṃ सतोतारंstotāraṃ याभिरावतंyābhirāvataṃ ताभिर.tābhir. .... ||
Whereby the cloud, ye Bounteous Givers, shed sweet rain for Dīrghaśravas, for the merchant Auśija,
Wherewith ye helped Kakṣīvān, singer of your praise,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.11
याभीyābhī रसांrasāṃ कषोदसोद्नःkṣodasodnaḥ पिपिन्वथुरनश्वंpipinvathuranaśvaṃ याभीyābhī रथमावतंrathamāvataṃ जिषेjiṣe |

याभिस्त्रिशोकyābhistriśoka उस्रियाusriyā उदाजतudājata ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye made Rasā swell full with water-floods, and urged to victory the car without a horse;
Wherewith Triśoka drove forth his recovered cows,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.12
याभिःyābhiḥ सूर्यंsūryaṃ परियाथःpariyāthaḥ परावतिparāvati मन्धातारंmandhātāraṃ कषैत्रपत्येष्वावतमkṣaitrapatyeṣvāvatam |

याभिर्विप्रंyābhirvipraṃ परpra भरद्वाजमावतंbharadvājamāvataṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye, compass round the Sun when far away, strengthened Mandhātar in his tasks as lord of lands,
And to sage Bharadvāja gave protecting help,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.13
याभिर्महामतिथिग्वंyābhirmahāmatithighvaṃ कशोजुवंkaśojuvaṃ दिवोदासंdivodāsaṃ शम्बरहत्यावतमśambarahatyaāvatam |

याभिःyābhiḥ पूर्भिद्येpūrbhidye तरसदस्युमावतंtrasadasyumāvataṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith, when Śambara was slain, ye guarded well great Atithigva, Divodāsa, Kaśoju,
And Trasadasyu when the forts were shattered down,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.14
याभिर्वम्रंyābhirvamraṃ विपिपानमुपस्तुतंvipipānamupastutaṃ कलिंkaliṃ याभिर्वित्तजानिंyābhirvittajāniṃ दुवस्यथःduvasyathaḥ |

याभिर्व्यश्वमुतyābhirvyaśvamuta पर्थिमावतंpṛthimāvataṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye honoured the great drinker Vamra, and Upastuta and Kali when he gained his wife,
And lent to Vyaśva and to Pṛthi favouring help,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.15
याभिर्नराyābhirnarā शयवेśayave याभिरत्रयेyābhiratraye याभिःyābhiḥ पुराpurā मनवेmanave गातुमीषथुःghātumīṣathuḥ |

याभिःyābhiḥ शारीराजतंśārīrājataṃ सयूमरश्मयेsyūmaraśmaye ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith, O Heroes, ye vouchsafed deliverance to Śayu, Atri, and to Manu long ago;
Wherewith ye shot your shafts in Syūmaraśmi's cause.—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.16
याभिःyābhiḥ पठर्वाpaṭharvā जठरस्यjaṭharasya मज्मनाग्निर्नादीदेच्चितmajmanāghnirnādīdeccita इद्धोiddho अज्मन्नाajmannā |

याभिःyābhiḥ शर्यातमवथोśaryātamavatho महाधनेmahādhane ताभिर..tābhir.. .. ||
Wherewith Paṭharvā, in his majesty of form, shone in his course like to a gathered kindled fire;
Wherewith ye helped Śāryāta in the mighty fray,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.17
याभिरङगिरोyābhiraṅghiro मनसाmanasā निरण्यथो.अग्रंniraṇyatho.aghraṃ गछथोghachatho विवरेvivare गोर्णसःghoarṇasaḥ |

याभिर्मनुंyābhirmanuṃ शूरमिषाśūramiṣā समावतंsamāvataṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith, Aṅgirases! ye triumphed in your heart, and onward went to liberate the flood of milk;
Wherewith ye helped the hero Manu with new strength,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.18
याभिःyābhiḥ पत्नीर्विमदायpatnīrvimadāya नयूहथुराnyūhathurā घgha वाvā याभिररुणीरशिक्षतमyābhiraruṇīraśikṣatam |

याभिःyābhiḥ सुदासsudāsa ऊहथुःūhathuḥ सुदेव्यंsudevyaṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye brought a wife for Vimada to wed, wherewith ye freely gave the ruddy cows away;
Wherewith ye brought the host of kind Gods to Sudās—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.19
याभिःyābhiḥ शन्तातीśantātī भवथोbhavatho ददाशुषेdadāśuṣe भुज्युंbhujyuṃ याभिरवथोyābhiravatho याभिरध्रिगुमyābhiradhrighum |

ओम्यावतींomyāvatīṃ सुभरांsubharāṃ रतस्तुभंṛtastubhaṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye bring great bliss to him who offers gifts, wherewith ye have protected Bhujyu, Adhrigu,
And good and gracious Subharā and Ṛtastup,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.20
याभिःyābhiḥ कर्शानुमसनेkṛśānumasane दुवस्यथोduvasyatho जवेjave याभिर्यूनोyābhiryūno अर्वन्तमावतमarvantamāvatam |

मधुmadhu परियंpriyaṃ भरथोbharatho यतyat सरड्भ्यस्ताभिर...saraḍbhyastābhir... ||
Wherewith ye served Kṛśānu where the shafts were shot, and helped the young man's horse to swiftness in the race;
Wherewith ye bring delicious honey to the bees,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.21
याभिर्नरंyābhirnaraṃ गोषुयुधंghoṣuyudhaṃ नर्षाह्येnṛṣāhye कषेत्रस्यkṣetrasya साताsātā तनयस्यtanayasya जिन्वथःjinvathaḥ |

याभीyābhī रथानवथोrathānavatho याभिरर्वतस्ताभिर...yābhirarvatastābhir... ||
Wherewith ye speed the hero as he fights for kine in hero battle, in the strife for land and sons,
Wherewith ye safely guard his horses and his car,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins with those aids.
1.112.22
याभिःyābhiḥ कुत्समार्जुनेयंkutsamārjuneyaṃ शतक्रतूśatakratū परpra तुर्वीतिंturvītiṃ परpra चca दभीतिमावतमdabhītimāvatam |

याभिर्ध्वसन्तिंyābhirdhvasantiṃ पुरुषन्तिमावतंpuruṣantimāvataṃ ताभिर...tābhir... ||
Wherewith ye, Lords of Hundred Powers, helped Kutsa, son of Ārjuni, gave Turvīti and Dabhīti strength,
Favoured Dhvasanti and lent Puruṣanti help,—Come hither unto us, O Aśvins, with those aids.
1.112.23
अप्नस्वतीमश्विनाapnasvatīmaśvinā वाचमस्मेvācamasme कर्तंkṛtaṃ नोno दस्राdasrā वर्षणाvṛṣaṇā मनीषामmanīṣām |

अद्यूत्ये.अवसेadyūtye.avase निni हवयेhvaye वांvāṃ वर्धेvṛdhe चca नोno भवतंbhavataṃ वाजसातौvājasātau ||
Make ye our speech effectual, O ye Aśvins, and this our hymn, ye mighty Wonder-Workers.
In luckless game I call on you for succour: strengthen us also on the field of battle.
1.112.24
दयुभिरक्तुभिःdyubhiraktubhiḥ परिpari पातमस्मानरिष्टेभिरश्विनाpātamasmānariṣṭebhiraśvinā सौभगेभिःsaubhaghebhiḥ |

तनtan नोno ...... ||
With, undiminished blessings, O ye Aśvins, for evermore both night and day protect us.
This prayer of ours may Varuṇa grant, and Mitra, and Aditi and Sindhu, Earth and Heaven.
1.112.25
