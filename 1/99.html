HYMN XCIX. AGNI.

जातवेदसेjātavedase सुनवामsunavāma सोममरातीयतोsomamarātīyato निni दहातिdahāti वेदःvedaḥ |

सsa नःnaḥ पर्षदतिparṣadati दुर्गाणिdurghāṇi विश्वाviśvā नावेवnāveva सिन्धुंsindhuṃ दुरितात्यग्निःduritātyaghniḥ ||
FOR Jātavedas let us press the Soma: may he consume the wealth of the malignant.
May Agni carry us through all our troubles, through grief as in a boat across the river.
