HYMN CLXXX. AŚVINS.

युवोyuvo रजांसिrajāṃsi सुयमासोsuyamāso अश्वाaśvā रथोratho यदyad वांvāṃ पर्यर्णांसिदियतparyarṇāṃsidiyat |

हिरण्ययाhiraṇyayā वांvāṃ पवयहpavayah परुषायनpruṣāyan मध्वःmadhvaḥ पिबन्ताpibantā उषसःuṣasaḥ सचेथेsacethe ||
LIGHTLY your coursers travel through the regions when round the sea of air your car is flying.
Your golden fellies scatter drops of moisture: drinking the sweetness ye
attend
the Mornings.
1.180.1
युवमत्यस्यावyuvamatyasyāva नक्षथोnakṣatho यदyad विपत्मनोvipatmano नर्यस्यnaryasya परयज्योःprayajyoḥ |

सवसाsvasā यदyad वांvāṃ विश्वगूर्तीviśvaghūrtī भरातिbharāti वाजायेट्टेvājāyeṭṭe मधुपाविषेmadhupāviṣe चca ||
Ye as ye travel overtake the Courser who flies apart, the Friend of man, most holy.
The prayer is that the Sister may convey you, all praised, meath-drinkers! to support and strengthen.
1.180.2
युवंyuvaṃ पयpaya उस्रियायामधत्तंusriyāyāmadhattaṃ पक्वमामायामवpakvamāmāyāmava पूर्व्यंगोःpūrvyaṃghoḥ |

अन्तर्यदantaryad वनिनोvanino वांvāṃ रतप्सूṛtapsū हवारोhvāro नna शुचिर्यजतेśuciryajate हविष्मानhaviṣmān ||
Ye have deposited, matured within her, in the raw cow the first milk of the milch-cow,
Which the bright offerer, shining like a serpent mid trees, presents to you whose form is perfect.
1.180.3
युवंyuvaṃ हha घर्मंgharmaṃ मधुमन्तमत्रये.अपोmadhumantamatraye.apo नna कषोदो.अव्र्णीतमेषेkṣodo.avṛṇītameṣe |

तदtad वांvāṃ नरावश्विनाnarāvaśvinā पश्वैष्टीpaśvaiṣṭī रथ्येवrathyeva चक्राcakrā परतिprati यन्तिyanti मध्वःmadhvaḥ ||
Ye made the fierce heat to be full of sweetness for Atri at his wish, like streaming water.
Fire-offering thence is yours, O Aśvins, Heroes: your car-wheels speed to us like springs of honey.
1.180.4
आā वांvāṃ दानायdānāya वव्र्तीयvavṛtīya दस्राdasrā गोरोहेणghoroheṇa तौग्र्योtaughryo नna जिव्रिःjivriḥ |

अपःapaḥ कषोणीkṣoṇī सचतेsacate माहिनाmāhinā वांvāṃ जूर्णोjūrṇo वामक्षुरंहसोvāmakṣuraṃhaso यजत्राyajatrā ||
Like Tugra's ancient son may I, ye Mighty, bring you to give your gifts with milk-oblations.
Your greatness compasseth Earth, Heaven, and Waters: decayed for you is sorrow's net, ye Holy.
1.180.5
निni यदyad युवेथेyuvethe नियुतःniyutaḥ सुदानूsudānū उपupa सवधाभिःsvadhābhiḥ सर्जथःsṛjathaḥ पुरन्धिमpurandhim |

परेषदpreṣad वेषदveṣad वातोvāto नna सूरिराsūrirā महेmahe ददेdade सुव्रतोनsuvratona वाजमvājam ||
When, Bounteous Ones, ye drive your yoked team downward, ye send, by your own natures, understanding.
Swift as the wind let the prince please and feast you: he, like a pious man, gains strength for increase.
1.180.6
वयंvayaṃ चिदcid धिdhi वांvāṃ जरितारःjaritāraḥ सत्याsatyā विपन्यामहेvipanyāmahe विvi पणिर्हितावानpaṇirhitāvān |

अधाadhā चिदcid धिdhi षमाश्विनावनिन्द्याṣmāśvināvanindyā पाथोpātho हिhi षमाव्र्षणावन्तिदेवमṣmāvṛṣaṇāvantidevam ||
For verily we truthful singers praise you the niggard trafficker is here excluded.
Now, even now do ye O blameless Aśvins, ye Mighty, guard the man whose God is near him.
1.180.7
युवंyuvaṃ चिदcid धिdhi षमाश्विनावनुṣmāśvināvanu दयूनdyūn विरुद्रस्यvirudrasya परस्रवणस्यसातौprasravaṇasyasātau |

अगस्त्योaghastyo नरांnarāṃ नर्षुnṛṣu परशस्तःpraśastaḥ काराधुनीवkārādhunīva चितयतcitayat सहस्रैःsahasraiḥ ||
You of a truth day after day, O Aśvins, that he might win the very plenteous torrent,
Agastya, famous among mortal heroes, roused with a thousand lauds like sounds of music.
1.180.8
परpra यदyad वहेथेvahethe महिनाmahinā रथस्यrathasya परpra सयन्द्राsyandrā याथोyātho मनुषोmanuṣo नna होताhotā |

धत्तंdhattaṃ सूरिभ्यsūribhya उतuta वva सवश्व्यंsvaśvyaṃ नासत्यnāsatya रयिषाचःrayiṣācaḥ सयामsyāma ||
When with the glory of your car ye travel, when we go speeding like the priest of mortals,
And give good horses to sacrificers, may we, Nāsatyas! gain our share of riches.
1.180.9
तंtaṃ वांvāṃ रथंrathaṃ वयमद्याvayamadyā हुवेमhuvema सतोमैरश्विनाstomairaśvinā सुवितायनव्यमsuvitāyanavyam |

अरिष्टनेमिंariṣṭanemiṃ परिpari दयामियानंdyāmiyānaṃ वि...vi... ||
With songs of praise we call to-day, O Aśvins, that your new chariot, for our own well-being,
That circles heaven with never-injured fellies. May we find strengthening food in full abundance.
