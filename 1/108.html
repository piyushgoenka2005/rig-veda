HYMN CVIII. INDRA-AGNI.

यya इन्द्राग्नीindrāghnī चित्रतमोcitratamo रथोratho वामभिvāmabhi विश्वानिviśvāni भुवनानिbhuvanāni चष्टेcaṣṭe |

तेनाtenā यातंyātaṃ सरथंsarathaṃ तस्थिवांसाथाtasthivāṃsāthā सोमस्यsomasya पिबतंpibataṃ सुतस्यsutasya ||
ON that most wondrous car of yours, O Indra and Agni, which looks round on all things living,
Take ye your stand and come to us together, and drink libations of the flowing Soma.
1.108.1
यावदिदंyāvadidaṃ भुवनंbhuvanaṃ विश्वमस्त्युरुव्यचाviśvamastyuruvyacā वरिमताvarimatā गभीरमghabhīram |

तावानयंtāvānayaṃ पातवेpātave सोमोsomo अस्त्वरमिन्द्राग्नीastvaramindrāghnī मनसेmanase युवभ्यामyuvabhyām ||
As vast as all this world is in its compass, deep as it is, with its far-stretching surface,
So let this Soma be, Indra and Agni, made for your drinking till your soul be sated.
1.108.2
चक्राथेcakrāthe हिhi सध्र्यंsadhryaṃ नामnāma भद्रंbhadraṃ सध्रीचीनाsadhrīcīnā वर्त्रहणाुतvṛtrahaṇāuta सथःsthaḥ |

ताविन्द्राग्नीtāvindrāghnī सध्र्यञ्चाsadhryañcā निषद्याniṣadyā वर्ष्णःvṛṣṇaḥ सोमस्यsomasya वर्षणाvṛṣaṇā वर्षेथामvṛṣethām ||
For ye have won a blessed name together: yea, with one aim ye strove, O Vṛtra-slayers.
So Indra-Agni, seated here together, pour in, ye Mighty Ones, the mighty Soma.
1.108.3
समिद्धेष्वग्निष्वानजानाsamiddheṣvaghniṣvānajānā यतस्रुचाyatasrucā बर्हिरुbarhiru तिस्तिराणाtistirāṇā |

तीव्रैःtīvraiḥ सोमैःsomaiḥ परिषिक्तेभिरर्वागेन्द्राग्नीpariṣiktebhirarvāghendrāghnī सौमनसायsaumanasāya यातमyātam ||
Both stand adorned, when fires are duly kindled, spreading the sacred grass, with lifted ladles.
Drawn by strong Soma juice poured forth around us, come, Indra-Agni, and display your favour.
1.108.4
यानीन्द्राग्नीyānīndrāghnī चक्रथुर्वीर्याणिcakrathurvīryāṇi यानिyāni रूपाण्युतrūpāṇyuta वर्ष्ण्यानिvṛṣṇyāni |

याyā वांvāṃ परत्नानिpratnāni सख्याsakhyā शिवानिśivāni तेभिःtebhiḥ सोमस्यsomasya पिबतंpibataṃ सुतस्यsutasya ||
The brave deeds ye have done, Indra and Agni, the forms ye have displayed and mighty exploits,
The ancient and auspicious bonds of friendship,—for sake of these drink of the flowing Soma.
1.108.5
यदब्रवंyadabravaṃ परथमंprathamaṃ वांvāṃ वर्णानो.अयंvṛṇāno.ayaṃ सोमोsomo असुरैर्नोasurairno विहव्यःvihavyaḥ |

तांtāṃ सत्यांsatyāṃ शरद्धामभ्याśraddhāmabhyā हिhi यातमथाyātamathā सोमस्यsomasya पिबतंpibataṃ सुतस्यsutasya ||
As first I said when choosing you, in battle we must contend with Asuras for this Soma.
So came ye unto this my true conviction, and drank libations of the flowing Soma.
1.108.6
यदिन्द्राग्नीyadindrāghnī मदथःmadathaḥ सवेsve दुरोणेduroṇe यदyad बरह्मणिbrahmaṇi राजनिrājani वायजत्राvāyajatrā |

अतःataḥ परिpari वर्षणावाvṛṣaṇāvā हिhi यातमथाyātamathā सोमस्यsomasya पिबतंpibataṃ सुतस्यsutasya ||
If in your dwelling, or with prince or Brahman, ye, Indra-Agni, Holy Ones, rejoice you,
Even from thence, ye mighty Lords, come hither, and drink libation of the flowing Soma.
1.108.7
यदिन्द्राग्नीyadindrāghnī यदुषुyaduṣu तुर्वशेषुturvaśeṣu यदyad दरुह्युष्वनुषुdruhyuṣvanuṣu पूरुषुpūruṣu सथःsthaḥ |

अतःataḥ ...... ||
If with, the Yadus, Turvaśas, ye sojourn, with Druhyus, Anus, Pūrus, Indra-Agni!
Even from thence, ye mighty Lords, come hither, and drink libations of the flowing Soma.
1.108.8
यदिन्द्राग्नीyadindrāghnī अवमस्यांavamasyāṃ पर्थिव्यांpṛthivyāṃ मध्यमस्यांmadhyamasyāṃ परमस्यामुतparamasyāmuta सथःsthaḥ |

अतःataḥ ...... ||
Whether, O Indra-Agni, ye be dwelling in lowest earth, in central, or in highest.
Even from thence, ye mighty Lords, come hither, and drink libations of the flowing Soma.
1.108.9
यदिन्द्राग्नीyadindrāghnī परमस्यांparamasyāṃ पर्थिव्यांpṛthivyāṃ मध्यमस्यामवमस्यामुतmadhyamasyāmavamasyāmuta सथःsthaḥ |

अतःataḥ ...... ||
Whether, O Indra-Agni, ye be dwelling in highest earth, in central, or in lowest,
Even from thence, ye mighty Lords, come hither, and drink libations of the flowing Soma.
1.108.10
यदिन्द्राग्नीyadindrāghnī दिविdivi षठोṣṭho यतyat पर्थिव्यांpṛthivyāṃ यतyat पर्वतेष्वोषधीष्वप्सुparvateṣvoṣadhīṣvapsu |

अतःataḥ ...... ||
Whether ye be in heaven, O Indra-Agni, on earth, on mountains, in the herbs, or waters,
Even from thence, ye mighty Lords, come hither, and drink libations of the flowing Soma.
1.108.11
यदिन्द्राग्नीyadindrāghnī उदिताuditā सूर्यस्यsūryasya मध्येmadhye दिवःdivaḥ सवधयाsvadhayā मादयेथेmādayethe |

अतःataḥ ...... ||
If, when the Sun to the mid-heaven hath mounted, ye take delight in food, O Indra-Agni,
Even from thence, ye mighty Lords, come hither, and drink libations of the flowing Soma.
1.108.12
एवेन्द्राग्नीevendrāghnī पपिवांसाpapivāṃsā सुतस्यsutasya विश्वास्मभ्यंviśvāsmabhyaṃ संsaṃ जयतन्धनानिjayatandhanāni |

तनtan नोno ...... ||
Thus having drunk your fill of our libation, win us all kinds of wealth, Indra and Agni.
This prayer of ours may Varuṇa grant, and Mitra, and Aditi and Sindhu, Earth and Heaven.
