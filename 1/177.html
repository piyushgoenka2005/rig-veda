HYMN CLXXVII. INDRA.

आā चर्षणिप्राcarṣaṇiprā वर्षभोvṛṣabho जनानांjanānāṃ राजाrājā कर्ष्टीनांkṛṣṭīnāṃ पुरुहूतpuruhūta इन्द्रःindraḥ |

सतुतःstutaḥ शरवस्यन्नवसोपśravasyannavasopa मद्रिगmadrigh युक्त्वाyuktvā हरीव्र्षणाharīvṛṣaṇā याह्यर्वांyāhyarvāṃ ||
THE Bull of men, who cherishes all people, King of the Races, Indra, called of many,
Fame-loving, praised, hither to me with succour turn having yoked both vigorous Bay Horses!
1.177.1
येye तेte वर्षणोvṛṣaṇo वर्षभासvṛṣabhāsa इन्द्रindra बरह्मयुजोbrahmayujo वर्षरथासोvṛṣarathāso अत्याःatyāḥ |

तानाtānā तिष्ठtiṣṭha तेभिराtebhirā याह्यर्वांyāhyarvāṃ हवामहेhavāmahe तवाtvā सुतsuta इन्द्रindra सोमेsome ||
Thy mighty Stallions, yoked by prayer, O Indra, thy. Coursers to thy mighty chariot harnessed,—
Ascend thou these, and borne by them come hither: with Soma juice outpoured, Indra, we call thee.
1.177.2
आā तिष्ठtiṣṭha रथंrathaṃ वर्षणंvṛṣaṇaṃ वर्षाvṛṣā तेte सुतःsutaḥ सोमःsomaḥ परिषिक्ताpariṣiktā मधूनिmadhūni |

युक्त्वाyuktvā वर्षभ्यांvṛṣabhyāṃ वर्षभvṛṣabha कषितीनांkṣitīnāṃ हरिभ्यांharibhyāṃ याहिyāhi परवतोपpravatopa मद्रिकmadrik ||
Ascend thy mighty car: the mighty Soma is poured for thee and sweets are sprinkled round us.
Come down to us-ward, Bull of human races, come, having harnessed them, with strong Bay Horses.
1.177.3
अयंayaṃ यज्ञोyajño देवयाdevayā अयंayaṃ मियेधmiyedha इमाimā बरह्मण्ययमिन्द्रbrahmaṇyayamindra सोमःsomaḥ |

सतीर्णंstīrṇaṃ बर्हिराbarhirā तुtu शक्रśakra परpra याहिyāhi पिबाpibā निषद्यविniṣadyavi मुचाmucā हरीharī इहiha ||
Here is God-reaching sacrifice, here the victim; here, Indra, are the prayers, here is the Soma.
Strewn is the sacred grass: come hither, Śakra; seat thee and drink: unyoke thy two Bay Coursers.
1.177.4
ओo सुष्टुतsuṣṭuta इन्द्रindra याह्यर्वांyāhyarvāṃ उपupa बरह्माणिbrahmāṇi मान्यस्यmānyasya कारोःkāroḥ |

विद्यामvidyāma वस्तोरवसाvastoravasā गर्णन्तोghṛṇanto वि...vi... ||
Come to us, Indra, come thou highly lauded to the devotions of the singer Māna.
Singing, may we find early through thy succour, may we find strengthening food in full abundance.
