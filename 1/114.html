HYMN CXIV. RUDRA.

इमाimā रुद्रायrudrāya तवसेtavase कपर्दिनेkapardine कषयद्वीरायkṣayadvīrāya परpra भरामहेbharāmahe मतीःmatīḥ |

यथाyathā शमसदśamasad दविपदेdvipade चतुष्पदेcatuṣpade विश्वंviśvaṃ पुष्टंग्रामेpuṣṭaṃghrāme अस्मिन्ननातुरमasminnanāturam ||
To the strong Rudra bring we these our songs of praise, to him the Lord of Heroes with the braided hair,
That it be well with all our cattle and our men, that in this village all be healthy and well-fed.
1.114.1
मर्ळाmṛḷā नोno रुद्रोतrudrota नोno मयसmayas कर्धिkṛdhi कषयद्वीरायkṣayadvīrāya नमसाnamasā विधेमतेvidhemate |

यच्छंyacchaṃ चca योश्चyośca मनुरायेजेmanurāyeje पिताpitā तदश्यामtadaśyāma तवरुद्रtavarudra परणीतिषुpraṇītiṣu ||
Be gracious unto us, O Rudra, bring us joy: thee, Lord of Heroes, thee with reverence will we serve.
Whatever health and strength our father Manu won by sacrifice may we, under thy guidance, gain.
1.114.2
अश्यामaśyāma तेte सुमतिंsumatiṃ देवयज्ययाdevayajyayā कषयद्वीरस्यkṣayadvīrasya तवtava रुद्रrudra मीढ्वःmīḍhvaḥ |

सुम्नायन्निदsumnāyannid विशोviśo अस्माकमाasmākamā चरारिष्टवीराcarāriṣṭavīrā जुहवामjuhavāma तेte हविःhaviḥ ||
By worship of the Gods may we, O Bounteous One, O Rudra, gain thy grace, Ruler of valiant men.
Come to our families, bringing them bliss: may we, whose heroes are uninjured, bring thee sacred gifts,
1.114.3
तवेषंtveṣaṃ वयंvayaṃ रुद्रंrudraṃ यज्ञसाधंyajñasādhaṃ वङकुंvaṅkuṃ कविमवसेkavimavase निह्वयामहेnihvayāmahe |

आरेāre अस्मदasmad दैव्यंdaivyaṃ हेळोheḷo अस्यतुasyatu सुमतिमिदsumatimid वयमस्याvayamasyā वर्णीमहेvṛṇīmahe ||
Hither we call for aid the wise, the wanderer, impetuous Rudra, perfecter of sacrifice.
May he repel from us the anger of the Gods: verily we desire his favourable grace.
1.114.4
दिवोdivo वराहमरुषंvarāhamaruṣaṃ कपर्दिनंkapardinaṃ तवेषंtveṣaṃ रूपंrūpaṃ नमसाnamasā निह्वयामहेnihvayāmahe |

हस्तेhaste बिभ्रदbibhrad भेषजाbheṣajā वार्याणिvāryāṇi शर्मśarma वर्मvarma छर्दिरस्मभ्यंchardirasmabhyaṃ यंसतyaṃsat ||
Him with the braided hair we call with reverence down, the wild-boar of the sky, the red, the dazzling shape.
May he, his hand filled full of sovran medicines, grant us protection, shelter, and a home secure.
1.114.5
इदंidaṃ पित्रेpitre मरुतामुच्यतेmarutāmucyate वचःvacaḥ सवादोःsvādoḥ सवादीयोsvādīyo रुद्रायrudrāya वर्धनमvardhanam |

रास्वाrāsvā चca नोno अम्र्तamṛta मर्तभोजनंmartabhojanaṃ तमनेtmane तोकायtokāya तनयायtanayāya मर्ळmṛḷa ||
To him the Maruts’ Father is this hymn addressed, to strengthen Rudra's might, a song more sweet than sweet.
Grant us, Immortal One, the food which mortals eat: be gracious unto me, my seed, my progeny.
1.114.6
माmā नोno महान्तमुतmahāntamuta माmā नोno अर्भकंarbhakaṃ माmā नna उक्षन्तमुतukṣantamuta मानmāna उक्षितमukṣitam |

माmā नोno वधीःvadhīḥ पितरंpitaraṃ मोतmota मातरंmātaraṃ माmā नःnaḥ परियास्तन्वोpriyāstanvo रुद्रrudra रीरिषःrīriṣaḥ ||
O Rudra, harm not either great or small of us, harm not the growing boy, harm not the full-grown man.
Slay not a sire among us, slay no mother here, and to our own dear bodies, Rudra, do not harm.
1.114.7
माmā नस्तोकेnastoke तनयेtanaye माmā नna आयौāyau माmā नोno गोषुghoṣu माmā नोno अश्वेषुरीरिषःaśveṣurīriṣaḥ |

वीरानvīrān माmā नोno रुद्रrudra भामितोbhāmito वधीर्हविष्मन्तःसदमितvadhīrhaviṣmantaḥsadamit तवाtvā हवामहेhavāmahe ||
Harm us not, Rudra, in our seed and progeny, harm us not in the living, nor in cows or steeds,
Slay not our heroes in the fury of thy wrath. Bringing oblations evermore we call to thee.
1.114.8
उपupa तेte सतोमानstomān पशुपाpaśupā इवाकरंivākaraṃ रास्वाrāsvā पितर्मरुतांpitarmarutāṃ सुम्नमस्मेsumnamasme |

भद्राbhadrā हिhi तेte सुमतिर्म्र्ळयत्तमाथाsumatirmṛḷayattamāthā वयमवvayamava इत्तेitte वर्णीमहेvṛṇīmahe ||
Even as a herdsman I have brought thee hymns of praise: O Father of the Maruts, give us happiness,
Blessed is thy most favouring benevolence, so, verily, do we desire thy saving help.
1.114.9
आरेāre तेte गोघ्नमुतghoghnamuta पूरुषघ्नंpūruṣaghnaṃ कषयद्वीरkṣayadvīra सुम्नमस्मेsumnamasme तेस्तुteastu |

मर्ळाmṛḷā चca नोno अधिadhi चca बरूहिbrūhi देवाधाdevādhā चca नःnaḥ शर्मśarma यछद्विबर्हाःyachadvibarhāḥ ||
Far be thy dart that killeth men or cattle: thy bliss be with us, O thou Lord of Heroes.
Be gracious unto us, O God, and bless us, and then vouchsafe us doubly-strong protection.
1.114.10
अवोचामavocāma नमोnamo अस्माasmā अवस्यवःavasyavaḥ शर्णोतुśṛṇotu नोno हवंhavaṃ रुद्रोrudro मरुत्वानmarutvān |

तनtan नोno ...... ||
We, seeking help, have spoken and adored him: may Rudra, girt by Maruts, hear our calling.
This prayer of ours may Varuṇa grant, and Mitra, and Aditi and Sindhu, Earth and Heaven.
1.114.11
