HYMN XVII. INDRA-VARUṆA

इन्द्रावरुणयोरहंindrāvaruṇayorahaṃ सम्राजोरवsamrājorava आā वर्णेvṛṇe |

ताtā नोno मर्ळातीद्र्शेmṛḷātaīdṛśe ||
I CRAVE help from the Imperial Lords, from Indra-Varuṇa; may they
Both favour one of us like me.
1.17.1
गन्ताराghantārā हिhi सथो.अवसेstho.avase हवंhavaṃ विप्रस्यviprasya मावतःmāvataḥ |

धर्ताराचर्षणीनामdhartārācarṣaṇīnām ||
Guardians of men, ye ever come with ready succour at the call
Of every singer such as I.
1.17.2
अनुकामंanukāmaṃ तर्पयेथामिन्द्रावरुणtarpayethāmindrāvaruṇa रायrāya आā |

ताtā वांvāṃ नेदिष्ठमीमहेnediṣṭhamīmahe ||
Sate you, according to your wish, O Indra-Varuṇa, with wealth:
Fain would we have you nearest us.
1.17.3
युवाकुyuvāku हिhi शचीनांśacīnāṃ युवाकुyuvāku सुमतीनामsumatīnām |

भूयामbhūyāma वाजदाव्नामvājadāvnām ||
May we be sharers of the powers, sharers of the benevolence
Of you who give strength bounteously.
1.17.4
इन्द्रःindraḥ सहस्रदाव्नांsahasradāvnāṃ वरुणःvaruṇaḥ शंस्यानामśaṃsyānām |

करतुर्भवत्युक्थ्यःkraturbhavatyukthyaḥ ||
Indra and Varuṇa, among givers of thousands, meet for praise,
Are Powers who merit highest laud.
1.17.5
तयोरिदवसाtayoridavasā वयंvayaṃ सनेमsanema निni चca धीमहिdhīmahi |

सयादुतsyāduta पररेचनमprarecanam ||
Through their protection may we gain great store of wealth, and heap it up
Enough and still to spare, be ours.
1.17.6
इन्द्रावरुणindrāvaruṇa वामहंvāmahaṃ हुवेhuve चित्रायcitrāya राधसेrādhase |

अस्मानasmān सुsu जिग्युषसjighyuṣas कर्तमkṛtam ||
O Indra-Varuṇa, on you for wealth in many a form I call:
Still keep ye us victorious.
1.17.7
इन्द्रावरुणindrāvaruṇa नूnū नुnu वांvāṃ सिषासन्तीषुsiṣāsantīṣu धीष्वाdhīṣvā |

अस्मभ्यंasmabhyaṃ शर्मśarma यछतमyachatam ||
O Indra-Varuṇa, through our songs that seek to win you to ourselves,
Give us at once your sheltering help.
1.17.8
परpra वामश्नोतुvāmaśnotu सुष्टुतिरिन्द्रावरुणsuṣṭutirindrāvaruṇa यांyāṃ हुवेhuve |

यांyāṃ रधाथेṛdhāthe सधस्तुतिमsadhastutim ||
O Indra-Varuṇa, to you may fair praise which I offer come,
Joint eulogy which ye dignify.
1.17.9
