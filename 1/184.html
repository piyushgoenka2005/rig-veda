HYMN CLXXXIV. AŚVINS.

ताtā वामद्यvāmadya तावपरंtāvaparaṃ हुवेमोछन्त्यामुषसिhuvemochantyāmuṣasi वह्निरुक्थैःvahnirukthaiḥ |

नासत्याnāsatyā कुहkuha चितcit सन्तावर्योsantāvaryo दिवोdivo नपाताnapātā सुदास्तरायsudāstarāya ||
LET us invoke you both this day and after the priest is here with lauds when morn is breaking:
Nāsatyas, wheresoe’er ye be, Heaven's Children, for him who is more liberal than the godless.
1.184.1
अस्मेasme ऊū षुṣu वर्षणाvṛṣaṇā मादयेथामुतmādayethāmut पणीन्र्हतमूर्म्याpaṇīnrhatamūrmyā मदन्ताmadantā |

शरुतंśrutaṃ मेme अछोक्तिभिर्मतीनामेष्टाachoktibhirmatīnāmeṣṭā नराnarā निचेतारचnicetāraca कर्णैःkarṇaiḥ ||
With us, ye Mighty, let yourselves be joyful, glad in our stream of Soma slay the niggards.
Graciously hear my hymns and invitations, marking, O Heroes, with your cars my longing.
1.184.2
शरियेśriye पूषन्निषुक्र्तेवpūṣanniṣukṛteva देवाdevā नासत्याnāsatyā वहतुंvahatuṃ सूर्यायाःsūryāyāḥ |

वच्यन्तेvacyante वांvāṃ ककुहाkakuhā अप्सुapsu जाताjātā युगाyughā जूर्णेवjūrṇeva वरुणस्यvaruṇasya भूरेःbhūreḥ ||
Nāsatyas, Pūṣans, ye as Gods for glory arranged and set in order Sūrya's bridal.
Your giant steeds move on, sprung from the waters, like ancient times of Varuṇa the Mighty.
1.184.3
अस्मेasme साsā वांvāṃ माध्वीmādhvī रातिरस्तुrātirastu सतोमंstomaṃ हिनोतंhinotaṃ मान्यस्यmānyasya कारोःkāroḥ |

अनुanu यदyad वांvāṃ शरवस्याśravasyā सुदानूsudānū सुवीर्यायsuvīryāya चर्षणयोमदन्तिcarṣaṇayomadanti ||
Your grace be with us, ye who love sweet juices: further the hymn sung by the poet Māna,
When men are joyful in your glorious actions, to win heroic strength, ye Bounteous Givers.
1.184.4
एषeṣa वांvāṃ सतोमोstomo अश्विनावकारिaśvināvakāri मानेभिर्मघवानाmānebhirmaghavānā सुव्र्क्तिsuvṛkti |

यातंyātaṃ वर्तिस्तनयायvartistanayāya तमनेtmane चागस्त्येcāghastye नासत्याnāsatyā मदन्ताmadantā ||
This praise was made, O liberal Lords, O Aśvins, for you with fair adornment by the Mānas.
Come to our house for us and for our children, rejoicing, O Nāsatyas, in Agastya.
1.184.5
अतारिष्मatāriṣma ...... ||
We have passed o’er the limit of this darkness: our praise hath been bestowed on you, O Aśvins.
Come hitherward by paths which Gods have travelled. may we find strengthening food in full abundance.
