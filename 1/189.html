HYMN CLXXXIX. AGNI.

अग्नेaghne नयnaya सुपथाsupathā रायेrāye अस्मानasmān विश्वानिviśvāni देवdeva वयुनानिvayunāni विद्वानvidvān |

युयोध्यस्मज्जुहुराणमेनोyuyodhyasmajjuhurāṇameno भूयिष्ठांbhūyiṣṭhāṃ तेte नमौक्तिंविधेमnamauktiṃvidhema ||
BY goodly paths lead us to riches, Agni, God who knowest every sacred duty.
Remove the sin that makes us stray and wander. most ample adoration will we bring thee.
1.189.1
अग्नेaghne तवंtvaṃ पारयाpārayā नव्योnavyo अस्मानasmān सवस्तिभिरतिsvastibhirati दुर्गाणिdurghāṇi विश्वाviśvā |

पुश्चpuśca पर्थ्वीpṛthvī बहुलाbahulā नna उर्विurvi भवाbhavā तोकायtokāya तनयायtanayāya शंśaṃ योःyoḥ ||
Lead us anew to happiness, O Agni; lead us beyond all danger and affliction.
Be unto us a wide broad ample castle bless, prosper on their way our sons and offspring.
1.189.2
अग्नेaghne तवमस्मदtvamasmad युयोध्यमीवाyuyodhyamīvā अनग्नित्राanaghnitrā अभ्यमन्तabhyamanta कर्ष्टीःkṛṣṭīḥ |

पुनरस्मभ्यंpunarasmabhyaṃ सुवितायsuvitāya देवdeva कषांkṣāṃ विश्वेभिरम्र्तेभिर्यजत्रviśvebhiramṛtebhiryajatra ||
Far from us, Agni, put thou all diseases let them strike lauds that have no saving Agni.
God, make our home again to be a blessing, with all the Immortal Deities, O Holy.
1.189.3
पाहिpāhi नोno अग्नेaghne पायुभिरजस्रैरुतpāyubhirajasrairuta परियेpriye सदनsadana आā शुशुक्वानśuśukvān |

माmā तेte भयंbhayaṃ जरितारंjaritāraṃ यविष्ठyaviṣṭha नूनंnūnaṃ विदनvidan मापरंmāparaṃ सहस्वःsahasvaḥ ||
Preserve us, Agni, with perpetual succour, refulgent in the dwelling which thou lovest.
O Conqueror, most youthful, let no danger touch him who praises thee to-day or after.
1.189.4
माmā नोno अग्ने.अवaghne.ava सर्जोsṛjo अघायाविष्यवेaghāyāviṣyave रिपवेripave दुछुनायैduchunāyai |

मादत्वतेmādatvate दशतेdaśate मादतेmādate नोno माmā रीषतेrīṣate सहसावनsahasāvan पराparā दाःdāḥ ||
Give not us up a prey to sin, O Agni, the greedy enemy that brings us trouble;
Not to the fanged that bites, not to the toothless: give not us up, thou Conqueror, to the spoiler.
1.189.5
विvi घgha तवावानtvāvān रतजातṛtajāta यंसदyaṃsad गर्णानोghṛṇāno अग्नेaghne तन्वेtanve वरूथमvarūtham |

विश्वादviśvād रिरिक्षोरुतririkṣoruta वाvā निनित्सोरभिह्रुतामसिninitsorabhihrutāmasi हिhi देवdeva विष्पटviṣpaṭ ||
Such as thou art, born after Law, O Agni when lauded give protection to our bodies,
From whosoever would reproach or injure: for thou, God, rescuest from all oppression.
1.189.6
तवंtvaṃ तानग्नtānaghna उभयानिवubhayāniv विद्वानvidvān वेषिveṣi परपित्वेprapitve मनुषोmanuṣo यजत्रyajatra |

अभिपित्वेabhipitve मनवेmanave शास्योśāsyo भूर्मर्म्र्जेन्यbhūrmarmṛjenya उशिग्भिर्नाक्रःuśighbhirnākraḥ ||
Thou, well discerning both these classes, comest to men at early morn, O holy Agni.
Be thou obedient unto man at evening, to be adorned, as keen, by eager suitors.
1.189.7
अवोचामavocāma निवचनान्यस्मिनnivacanānyasmin मानस्यmānasya सूनुःsūnuḥ सहसानेsahasāne अग्नौaghnau |

वयंvayaṃ सहस्रंsahasraṃ रषिभिःṛṣibhiḥ सनेमsanema वि...vi... ||
To him have we addressed our pious speeches, I, Māna's son, to him victorious Agni.
May we gain countless riches with the sages. May we find strengthening food in full abundance.
1.189.8
