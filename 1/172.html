HYMN CLXXII. MARUTS.

चित्रोcitro वो.अस्तुvo.astu यामश्चित्रyāmaścitra ऊतीūtī सुदानवःsudānavaḥ |

मरुतोmaruto अहिभानवःahibhānavaḥ ||
WONDERFUL let your coming be, wondrous with help, ye Bounteous Ones,
Maruts, who gleam as serpents gleam.
1.172.1
आरेāre साsā वःvaḥ सुदानवोsudānavo मरुतmaruta रञ्जतीṛñjatī शरुःśaruḥ |

आरेāre अश्माaśmā यमस्यथyamasyatha ||
Far be from us, O Maruts, ye free givers, your impetuous shaft;
Far from us be the stone ye hurl.
1.172.2
तर्णस्कन्दस्यtṛṇaskandasya नुnu विशःviśaḥ परिpari वर्ङकतvṛṅkta सुदानवःsudānavaḥ |

ऊर्ध्वानūrdhvān नःnaḥ कर्तkarta जीवसेjīvase ||
O Bounteous Givers, touch ye not, O Maruts, Tṛṇskanda's folk;
Lift ye us up that we may live.
1.172.3
